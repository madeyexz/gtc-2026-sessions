## Abstract
Discover how we realize the vision in quantum-HPC integration that "the sum is greater than its parts" by integrating the Fugaku supercomputer and NVIDIA GB200 clusters with superconducting and ion-trap QPUs in our JHPC-Quantum project. Our tightly coupled software stack, including the low-latency RPC middleware, orchestrates heterogeneous resources, and when combined with our novel quantum-HPC hybrid algorithms, we achieve utility-scale results in realistic problems in areas such as advanced quantum chemistry and computational fluid dyamics using hybrid algorithms—not just those that are advantageous to quantum. With rapid advances in both quantum and HPC systems, these will likely lead to true quantum advantages in real applications in the very near future.
