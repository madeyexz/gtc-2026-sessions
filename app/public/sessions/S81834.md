### Shankar Krishnamoorthy (Co-Presenter)

## Abstract
Join Samsung experts to explore how AI and digital twins are reshaping semiconductor manufacturing—from electronic design automation (EDA) and computational lithography to the design and operation of advanced fabrication plants. Learn how Samsung builds solutions with NVIDIA CUDA-X libraries, including cuLitho, to achieve up to 20x speedups in key manufacturing processes. Explore how they leverage NVIDIA Omniverse libraries to build large-scale industrial digital twins to conduct closed loop simulations and predict and optimize fab scheduling, maintenance, and yield. Discover how AI agents, built on NVIDIA Cosmos world foundation models, are driving chip, process, and system-wide optimization and innovation. Learn how Samsung’s AI Factory, powered by over 50,000 NVIDIA GPUs, makes all of this possible.
