## Abstract
Learn how Medivis is revolutionizing surgical care with the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center (UPMC) and MAIA, their groundbreaking extended reality (XR) and AI platform. Discover how MAIA gives surgeons real-time, conversational access to vast medical data through an advanced retrieval-augmented generation system. We'll also showcase its powerful image recognition capabilities powered by NVIDIA Nemo Agent Toolkit and Cosmos, delivering the next generation of surgical precision and care.
