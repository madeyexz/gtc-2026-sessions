## Abstract
The retail AI hype cycle is over—now it’s about results. Leading retailers are moving beyond experimental chatbots to deploy AI agents that complete work end to end. From resolving maintenance tickets and managing in-store promotions to routing human resources cases and handling customer issues without human hand-offs, these agents drive bottom-line savings and top-line growth.

In this session, we’ll explore the inflection point retail faces with AI—why those who move now will lead, and what sets successful adopters apart. See how ServiceNow customers deploy AI agents, powered by NVIDIA’s AI infrastructure, to boost efficiency and deliver results. We’ll share what’s working and how AI agents drive workflow automation that solves challenges in store operations, promotions, and service—turning talk into tangible results.
