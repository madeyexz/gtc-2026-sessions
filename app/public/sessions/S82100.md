## Speakers
### Dr. Shi Zhe (Presenter)
> Zhe Shi is the chief digital officer of Foxconn Technology Group and head of the Smart Manufacturing Platform, as well as CEO of Maxnerva. He focuses on building the group's "Lighthouse Factory" clusters and deploying internet-of-things and digital supply chain technologies.

## Abstract
In this session, we will share how Foxconn leverages its own advantages in the era of Physical AI, together with Project Genesis, a group-level centralized AI platform initiative, to cooperate with global ecological partners such as NVIDIA, BCG, ABB, Intrinsic, Siemens, etc., to build “AI Factory” and “Data Factory,” and dive into the application of various automation and robots in production lines that integrate the concept of Physical AI technology to jointly promote the transformation of Foxconn manufacturing into “AI intensive.”
