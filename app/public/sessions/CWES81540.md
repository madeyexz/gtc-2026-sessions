## Abstract
Engage directly with the Megatron Core MoE development team to explore mixture of experts (MoE) training at scale. This interactive session offers a unique opportunity to discuss the latest advancements in MoE training framework, roadmap, and best practices with the engineers who build it. We'll cover how to train state-of-the-art models like DeepSeek-V3 and Qwen, and share strategies for achieving optimal performance on the latest Grace Blackwell hardware.

We'll dive into key features such as leveraging FP8 for accelerated training, implementing efficient expert parallelism with DeepEP/HybridEP, maximizing communication efficiency with 1F1B EP Overlap, and minimizing memory footprint. Whether you're building the next foundation model or optimizing an existing training pipeline, this session delivers actionable insights to maximize throughput and harness the full power of Megatron Core.

Important: Connect With the Experts sessions are interactive sessions that give you a unique opportunity to meet, in either a group or one-on-one setting, with the minds behind NVIDIA’s products and research to get your questions answered.
