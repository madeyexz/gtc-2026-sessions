## Abstract
We introduce GreenMind, a large language model designed for complex reasoning through chain-of-thought data and self-correction cycles. By leveraging the GRPO strategy from NVIDIA NEMO for fine-tuning, we guide the model to generate logically coherent responses, deliver informative answers, and offer intuitive explanations. These features provide value not only to end users, but also to ongoing research aimed at improving data quality and sampling techniques.
