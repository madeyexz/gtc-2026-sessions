## Abstract
Learn how Fincantieri, one of the world’s largest shipbuilders, designed and implemented humanoid robots engineered to perform complex tasks in dangerous shipyard environments characterized by high variability and stringent safety requirements. Leveraging NVIDIA’s simulation and AI orchestration solutions, EY helped Fincantieri accelerate workflow automation and reduce manual interventions. Dive into Fincantieri’s strategic vision and understand the challenges for integrating humanoids into an industrial environment.
