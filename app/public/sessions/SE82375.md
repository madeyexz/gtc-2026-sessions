# Vibe Hack: Rapid Prototyping, From Idea to Reality

`Public Special Event` `Technical - Intermediate`

## Schedule
- **Thursday, 2026-03-19** 08:00 AM - 12:00 PM @ Guildhouse

## Abstract
Experience the power of "Vibe Coding"—where momentum drives creation. In this accessible three-hour session, we are removing complex roadmaps to focus on rapid ideation and execution. Whether you are a business leader, a student, or a seasoned developer, you will use NVIDIA cutting-edge AI models to turn concepts into working applications in real time. Collaborate with others, meet community members, and learn from others by building together. All teams will compete for the main prizes, with a special bounty category dedicated to best use of NVIDIA Nemotron models.

All entries are eligible to be featured in a Developer Showcase.
