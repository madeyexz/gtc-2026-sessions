## Abstract
Generating the photorealistic data and robust reasoning capabilities needed for machines to understand real-world complexity remains one of the biggest bottlenecks in physical AI. NVIDIA Cosmos world foundation models are at the core of AI models and agents that can perceive, understand, and adapt to the real-world like never before. Learn how developers can use Cosmos open models and tools to accelerate training, customize pipelines, and deploy faster across robotics, autonomous vehicles, and vision AI agents. This session will also explore how the latest Cosmos updates advance physics-aware learning, unlock simulation-to-real capabilities, and enable long-running agents and frameworks like OpenClaw to orchestrate continuous synthetic data generation and reasoning workflows at scale.
