## Abstract
AI factories are becoming mission‑critical, gigawatt‑scale infrastructure that must be engineered as a single, connected system. This session presents a “chip‑to‑grid” workflow focused on two key performance indicators: time‑to‑token and watts‑per‑token. 

It begins at the silicon layer, where AI‑native, GPU‑accelerated electronic design automation that utilizes Fuse and NVIDIA Nemotron accelerates design cycles. The workflow then extends to the factory, where Siemens Digital Twin Composer with NVIDIA Omniverse enables decision‑grade digital twins across power, cooling, and layout before construction. The session will conclude with an operational reference architecture that improves efficiency, resilience, and uptime. Join us to explore how digital twins redefine AI factory design, construction, and operations—creating a new level of operational excellence.
