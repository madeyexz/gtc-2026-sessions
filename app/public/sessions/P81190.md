# Enhance Production of Synthetic Radar Images From Geostationary Satellite Observations With Generative Diffusion Models

`Poster` `Technical - Intermediate` `Agentic AI / Generative AI`

## Speakers
### Yuguang Hu (Author)
> Yuguang Hu is an M.Phil. student in computer science at the University of Sydney. His research focuses on generative models.

## Abstract
Explore how generative diffusion models can transform geostationary satellite and lightning observations into realistic, high-resolution radar reflectivity imagery. This work introduces a probabilistic approach that improves key metrics and reproduces fine-scale storm structures, helping extend radar-like coverage to regions without operational radar systems.
