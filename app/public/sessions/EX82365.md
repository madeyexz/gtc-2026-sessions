# Making AI Agents Explainable for Manufacturing at Scale With Dataiku and NVIDIA (Presented by Dataiku)

`Sponsored Expo Theater` `Technical - Intermediate` `Developer Tools & Techniques`

## Schedule
- **Thursday, 2026-03-19** 12:40 PM - 12:55 PM @ SJCC Hall 1 Theater (L2)

## Speakers
### Hannes Hapke (Presenter)
> Hannes Hapke leads Dataiku's new Open Source Lab, with a focus on responsible AI, privacy, and AI explainability. His work centers on advancing open and trustworthy AI practices within the Dataiku ecosystem.

## Abstract
As manufacturers accelerate toward autonomous AI agents to reduce costly unplanned downtime, trust, transparency, and safety have become the critical blockers to adoption in high-stakes environments. This session shows how Dataiku and NVIDIA, together with the Puffin Inspector framework from Dataiku’s Open Source Lab, make AI-driven scheduling explainable, governed, and ready for real-world industrial orchestration today.
