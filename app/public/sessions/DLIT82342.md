## Abstract
Struggling to gather high-quality data for your vision language models? This hands-on training lab walks you through a complete synthetic data generation pipeline—from curation and augmentation to pseudo-annotation and quality control. You’ll learn how to transform any data into a high-quality dataset to post-train your vision language models (VLMs) for your domain-specific use cases.
