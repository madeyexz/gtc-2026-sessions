## Abstract
Financial institutions face a dilemma: build complex AI agents using the latest innovation, but without risking sensitive data leaving their secure perimeter. We will demonstrate how to bridge this gap by combining high-performance edge AI with a frictionless developer workflow leveraging open-source tooling. We explore how teams from Red Hat and Columbia University's Secure FinAI Lab utilized the HP ZGX Nano and the ZGX Toolkit to transform a bare-metal AI station into a fully governed "AI factory" in under 30 minutes—automating the setup and supporting the rapid development of innovative solutions to real-world financial problems. See how the ZGX Toolkit enables a seamless "desk-to-scale" workflow, where an Anti-Money Laundering (AML) detective agent achieves an 80% success in identifying financial crime topologies, is built, tested, and audited locally before being deployed to the cloud.
