## Abstract
Discover how spatial intelligence and pocket integration advance bioactivity prediction. We present DTIGN + LigoSpace, a next-gen framework that combines GeoREC — a geometric representation of spatial emptiness — and the Union-Pocket, a unified 3D structure merging multiple binding pockets of the same protein. Together, they capture how ligands truly fill and influence complex protein environments.

By integrating GeoREC for spatial representation, Union-Pocket for global context, and a pairwise learning strategy for accurate ligand ranking, our model delivers significant gains in bioactivity prediction. It learns not only if a ligand binds, but how completely it occupies its target, revealing the link between pocket filling and real-world potency. Accelerated on NVIDIA H200 GPUs, this approach achieves up to 96% faster inference.
