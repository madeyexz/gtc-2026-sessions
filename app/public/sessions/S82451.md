## Abstract
Despite fears of replacement, we may be entering a new wave of human creativity. AI systems increasingly generate content that comments on themselves or reveals emergent behavior. What happens when human judgment and machine intelligence blend together? This panel looks at emerging forms of expression that arise from human–AI collaboration, including outputs that feel unfamiliar or self‑reflective.
