## Abstract
AWS and Cox Automotive, powered by NVIDIA, demonstrate how AI agents are transforming vehicle operations from experimental tools into foundational business infrastructure. Cox Automotive built an agentic workflow in just four days using proprietary data and multi-agent orchestration to automate vehicle inspections, ensure accurate billing, and deliver consistent recommendations. By deploying real-time, collaborative AI agents, they transformed a manual, technician-driven process into a production-ready solution—reducing turnaround time from two days to under 30 minutes while boosting satisfaction for both customers and technicians.

This session showcases how AWS and NVIDIA enable rapid deployment of intelligent automation that delivers measurable business outcomes: faster cycle times, improved accuracy, and enhanced customer experience. AWS is built for industry.
