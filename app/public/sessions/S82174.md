## Abstract
Europe has built the foundation for having domestic AI compute positioned within the region, with projects across the U.K., Norway, Portugal, Iceland, and beyond, all using NVIDIA-accelerated infrastructure to keep compute, data, and value on European terms. In this 40-minute session, you’ll get a fast, practical view of how public–private partnerships are structuring these deployments—what’s working, what’s hard, and how to apply the same playbook in your own country or organization.
