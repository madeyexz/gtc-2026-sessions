## Abstract
Join us to discuss how AI is driving key breakthroughs in quantum computing. From discovering new quantum algorithms with generative AI and building AI-based quantum error correction decoders to enabling the low-latency integration of QPUs and AI supercomputing with NVQLink — whatever your interest in quantum computing, we'll show you how AI can accelerate your work. We’ll answer your questions about realizing these capabilities by architecting hybrid workflows with CUDA-Q, and show you how to leverage CUDA-Q Academic to prepare the next generation of experts to drive this convergence.

Important: Connect With the Experts sessions are interactive sessions that give you a unique opportunity to meet, in either a group or one-on-one setting, with the minds behind NVIDIA’s products and research to get your questions answered. Attendees can meet with experts on a first-come, first-served basis.
