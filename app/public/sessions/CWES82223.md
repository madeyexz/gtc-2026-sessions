## Abstract
Discover how to break the hardware bottleneck in industrial AI with CloudXR.js, the critical web-based extension of the NVIDIA CloudXR platform. This session will explore the plug-and-play nature of CloudXR.js, enabling effortless integration of diverse immersive devices—including Meta Quest and PICO—into high-performance streaming pipelines.
 
Learn how to operationalize this universal interface for large-scale robotics data collection and teleoperation. We will demonstrate how CloudXR.js bridges Isaac Lab simulations with wireless headsets to accelerate sim-to-real workflows. Beyond robotics, we will examine the platform’s impact on enterprise extended reality (XR) streaming in the automotive and manufacturing sectors, where browser-native accessibility is redefining how teams collaborate in immersive environments.
