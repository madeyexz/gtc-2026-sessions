## Abstract
Explore how we make quantum reinforcement learning for finance truly accessible using everyday GPUs. This poster demonstrates how you can train hybrid quantum–classical financial agents on NVIDIA RTX 3090s in just 20 hours, benchmarked on 18 years of Taiwan stock data, revealing how GPU-accelerated quantum simulation unlocks faster, fairer, and more inclusive financial AI research.
