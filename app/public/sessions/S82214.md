### Aakanksha Chowdhery (Panelist)

## Abstract
Join this session as Megatron core customers Reflection, Periodic Labs, XHS Rednote, and ByteDance describe their engineering use cases and best practices for pre-training their workloads. You will also hear about their contributions into this OSS project, features they leveraged for NVIDIA's latest hardware systems, and tuning for best performance at scale.
