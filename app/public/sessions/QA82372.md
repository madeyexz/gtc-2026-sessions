## Abstract
Several experts from the tools development and management teams will be available to talk about getting started, best practices, and advanced techniques for application debugging and optimization with NVIDIA developer tools. You can ask questions about anything from high-level concepts to specific tools details, and also discuss plans and suggestions for improving the tools in the future. We can provide a brief overview of the NVIDIA Nsight family of design, profiling, and debugging tools, including an introduction to the latest features and copilot integrations for developing CUDA compute, AI, and HPC applications on the newest platforms.

Important:Q&A with NVIDIA Experts are live, interactive sessions where attendees can ask questions directly to a panel of NVIDIA experts in a group setting. The experts address one question at a time for everyone in the room, so each answer is shared with all participants. This format is designed to deliver broadly relevant insights, turning every question into a chance for collective learning.
