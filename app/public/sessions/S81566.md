## Abstract
This session will introduce two use cases of NVIDIA platform adoption—one in climate prediction, and the other in smooth navigation.

Climate Prediction: Accurate climate prediction relies on capturing small-scale processes, such as clouds and turbulence, which remain elusive in traditional models. This talk presents the CliMA solution: a physics-AI hybrid Earth System Model built in Julia to run natively on NVIDIA GPUs. Leveraging GPU acceleration, researchers generate large ensembles to drive a "calibrate, emulate, sample" workflow. 

Generalized Navigation: the CUDA-accelerated Delta-Corridor framework utilizes Delta-Corridor spatial modeling to achieve reliable and smooth generalized navigation with Isaac and Cosmos. By applying continuous safety corridors, it ensures reliable navigation for mobile robots, boats, and autonomous vehicles in dynamic environments.
