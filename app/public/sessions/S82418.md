## Abstract
Self-driving vehicles represent the most immediate opportunity for AI to transform the physical world. This talk presents the technical architecture of Nuro’s autonomy platform, designed to generalize across geographies, use cases, and vehicles. Discover how Nuro’s end-to-end AI foundation model learns to drive safely in complex conditions. Learn how the Nuro Driver is integrated into passenger vehicles and how it is validated. See how this approach comes together through an applied example: Nuro’s global robotaxi program with Uber and Lucid.
