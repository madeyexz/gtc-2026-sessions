## Abstract
This session focuses on the deployment of agents in production, and hard engineering required to run models and agents at scale. Our experts will discuss the considerations of hosting open models using high-performance inference engines on your own infrastructure, as well as incorporating frontier models in your workflows. We discuss optimizing, inference speed, and reducing costs as well as implementing rigorous safety guardrails, along with evaluating and auditing models in production.
