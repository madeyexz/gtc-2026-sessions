## Abstract
New vision language models (VLMs) are transforming computer vision with scalable, reasoning zero-shot solutions. Learn about Cosmos Reason — a new open and fully customizable reasoning VLM for physical AI and robotics — that lets robots and vision AI agents reason like humans, using prior knowledge, physics understanding, and common sense to understand and act in the real world. In this lab, you'll build a video analytics AI agent using the NVIDIA Blueprint for Video Search and Summarization (VSS) with Cosmos Reason VLM.
 
Prerequisite(s):

Basic understanding of computer vision models.
