## Abstract
In today’s economy, accelerated computing is the engine behind AI, industrial optimization, and next-generation digital platforms. Yet demand for advanced chips continues to outpace supply, forcing enterprises to make sharper decisions about where their computing resources create the greatest impact. 

In this forward-looking conversation, sector leaders from industrial products, energy, and technology will explore how organizations can prioritize their accelerated computing resources across competing use cases. Rather than relying on static forecasts, the discussion will introduce a practical framework for aligning GPU deployment with business value, operational resilience, and broader societal needs. The goal is not simply to manage existing resources, but to ensure that compute capacity is directed toward the most transformative and economically meaningful applications. 

Designed to be timely and actionable, this session will help you move from reactive allocation to strategic prioritization in an environment defined by rapid technological change.
