## Abstract
The world was changed by creators who wrote the first kernels and turned raw potential into reality. Now, they are sharing the blueprints. Join us for an unfiltered look at CUDA’s impact with prolific creators who have redefined the boundaries of their fields. Hear the "aha" moments and war stories behind the software that shifted society. 

Since 2007, CUDA has served as the foundation for an accelerated computing revolution, enabling breakthroughs from genomic decoding to generative AI. This scale wasn't built on "killer apps" alone, but through decades of continuous evolution: maturing compilers, expanding libraries, and simplifying the path from idea to deployment. From CUDA-X to accessibility leaps like tile, the platform has evolved alongside its builders. Explore the journey from the first line of code to global scale and leave ready to build your own world-changing legacy.
