## Abstract
This session focuses on practical and system-level techniques to increase large language model throughput while reducing GPU cost and waste. It covers both training and inference optimization, with an emphasis on real-world bottlenecks such as memory bandwidth, kernel inefficiencies, batching limits, and underutilized GPUs.
