Retail and consumer packaged goods (CPG) are uniquely positioned to use AI to drive revenue growth and margin expansion. Leading retailers Walmart, Shopify, Amazon, and Lowe’s are partnering with NVIDIA to deploy AI at enterprise scale. 

NVIDIA’s vice president and general manager of retail will share how retailers can build an end-to-end, CEO-sponsored AI strategy and infrastructure that avoids siloed initiatives and delivers measurable return on investment. We'll cover how agentic AI and physical AI are transforming supply chains and store operations through simulation, robotics, and computer vision, while Agentic Commerce, AI Blueprints, and data science libraries unlock incremental ecommerce and retail media revenue. 

Learn best practices for deploying AI at scale with interoperability across any cloud and ownership of models trained on proprietary retail data. 

How to build an end-to-end AI strategy and infrastructure to ensure AI initiatives drive measurable ROI
Understand the importance of IP ownership of models trained with your proprietary data, and how to reduce inference cost.
How to deploy AI at scale across the entire enterprise
How to be in the driver seat with Agentic Commerce
