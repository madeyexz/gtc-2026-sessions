## Abstract
Hudson River Trading (HRT) is at the forefront of automated trading, processing vast amounts of data at lightning speeds. But their most impressive metric might be their efficiency. In this session, HRT engineers reveal how they are leveraging NVIDIA infrastructure to build the ultimate "AI factory."

Discover how to break the trade-off between speed and sustainability. We will cover the end-to-end journey of deploying AI-driven trading models that are faster, smarter, and greener. Learn how innovative data center design, renewable power strategies, and NVIDIA-optimized workloads combine to create a trading infrastructure that is resilient, autonomous, and environmentally conscious.
