## Abstract
The Aerial Framework has been designed from the ground up to meet the needs of 3GPP radio access networks (RANs)—signal processing workloads with microsecond latency requirements. It is a platform that unites research, test beds, and production deployments to solve development challenges for real-time applications.  

The session will give a hands-on tutorial to design algorithms in JAX/Torch and instantly deploy to real-time GPU pipelines with inline networking.  

GitHub: https://github.com/NVIDIA/aerial-framework
