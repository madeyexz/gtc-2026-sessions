## Abstract
Get a glimpse into the day-to-day life of a sovereign LLM model maker. Learn what drives them—specifically, the motivations behind creating sovereign models and the roles these models play in today’s AI ecosystem, particularly in light of emerging sovereignty requirements across Europe and around the world.

We'll also explore what it takes to build state-of-the-art models—from data preparation and quality assurance to large-scale distributed training, compression optimization, and deployment (using tools such as NeMo, Model Optimizer, Dynamo, TRT-LLM)—as well as how to develop a successful developer ecosystem around your models and products to ensure long-term sustainability.
