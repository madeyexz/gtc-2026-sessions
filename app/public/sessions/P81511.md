# AI-Augmented RFP Benchmarking Suite: Intelligent Performance Prediction for Heterogeneous Supercomputing Clusters

`Poster` `Technical - Intermediate` `Data Center / Cloud`

## Speakers
### Savitha Pareek (Author)
> A senior solutions architect at NVIDIA specializing in HPC/AI performance, large-scale benchmarking, and system design, Savitha is an inventor on multiple NVIDIA patents and author of 30-plus publications, with strong community and conference engagement.

## Abstract
We present an AI-assisted benchmarking method that predicts multi-node HPL performance using minimal measurements. The model achieves <3% error up to 32 nodes and detects uncertainty at higher scales, enabling faster and more efficient cluster sizing and request for proposal (RFP) evaluation.
