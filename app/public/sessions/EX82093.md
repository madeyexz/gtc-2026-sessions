## Abstract
As generative AI reshapes enterprise workloads, the rapid growth of vector databases introduces new challenges for scalability and performance. Kioxia Corp. addresses these challenges with AiSAQ, an innovative approximate nearest neighbor search (ANNS) algorithm that breaks traditional DRAM limitations by leveraging SSDs for search operations.  Now, AiSAQ adds powerful GPU utilization to vector indexing, transforming a time-consuming bottleneck into a highly accelerated workflow. In this session, discover how AiSAQ’s combination of SSD scalability and NVIDIA cuVS dramatically boosts throughput for demanding generative AI applications such as retrieval-augmented generation and semantic search. Gain insights into the benefits of integrating SSDs and GPUs, enabling robust, cost-effective solutions to meet the demands of today's AI-driven workloads.
