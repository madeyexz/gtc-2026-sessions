## Abstract
Meet with the experts at NVIDIA to learn how to accelerate vLLM inference performance on NVIDIA GPUs. Our experts at NVIDIA help make vLLM more performance and scalable. We cover a diversity of workloads running from DGX spark to NVIDIA GB200 NVL72. Join us for a deep dive in various optimization techniques, including speculative decoding, kernel performance, deployment recipes, reinforcement learning, and open-source software collaborations. Connect with us and share your vLLM use case, and together we'll explore how we can help improve the framework!

Important: Connect With the Experts sessions are interactive sessions that give you a unique opportunity to meet, in either a group or one-on-one setting, with the minds behind NVIDIA’s products and research to get your questions answered. Attendees can meet with experts on a first-come, first-served basis.
