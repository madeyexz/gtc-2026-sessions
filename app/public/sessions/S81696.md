## Abstract
In this talk, we highlight how emerging quantum computing technologies are poised to impact the National Energy Research Scientific Computing Center’s scientific workload. We show how NVIDIA’s hardware and software stack already enable next-generation quantum device and application simulations, and explain how NERSC’s future quantum roadmap will build on these capabilities.
