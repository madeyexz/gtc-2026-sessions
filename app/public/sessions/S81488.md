## Abstract
Physical AI is transforming how robots interact with the world. Building these advanced systems requires three computers: compute for training, advanced simulation for validation, and efficient edge deployment. With a simulation-first approach, robots can train across millions of scenarios, generalize faster, and adapt to real-world complexity. 
 
 In this technical deep-dive session, learn core robotics simulation workflows, from building virtual environments to configuring robots, sensors, and physics, using NVIDIA Isaac Sim and Isaac Lab, built with NVIDIA Omniverse libraries. You'll also explore how to generate and leverage synthetic data with NVIDIA Cosmos world foundation models to train and validate robot perception and control policies before deploying them in the real world.
