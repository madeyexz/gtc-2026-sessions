## Abstract
This poster emphasizes performance benefits of GPU direct storage. We ran the TRT LLM and compared it with and without GPUDirect Storage (GDS), and got better performance with GDS. This solves the major problem of loading data into GPU memory and avoids bounce buffer through CPU. Other GPU-accelerated applications can make use of a similar approach.
