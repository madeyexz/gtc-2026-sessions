## Abstract
Retail and consumer packaged goods (CPG) companies are uniquely positioned to use AI to drive revenue growth and margin expansion. Leading retailers around the world are partnering with NVIDIA to deploy AI at enterprise scale. NVIDIA’s VP and General Manager of Retail and CPG, Azita Martin, will share how retailers can build an end-to-end, CEO-sponsored AI strategy and infrastructure that avoids siloed initiatives and delivers measurable return on investment.

Discover how agentic AI and physical AI are transforming supply chains and store operations through simulation, robotics, and computer vision, while Agentic Commerce, AI Blueprints, and data science libraries unlock new opportunities in ecommerce and retail media. Learn best practices for deploying AI at scale with interoperability across any cloud and with full ownership of models trained on proprietary retail data.
