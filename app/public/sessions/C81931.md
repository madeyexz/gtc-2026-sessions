## Abstract
Ready to build the next generation of autonomous systems that perceive, reason, and act in the physical world? Join us for an in-depth look at NVIDIA's physical AI training and OpenUSD certification program at this year's GTC.
In this session, you'll discover how NVIDIA Omniverse libraries enable accelerated development across autonomous systems and identify a clear roadmap for earning professional OpenUSD certification for simulation workflows and physical AI data integration.
You'll learn:

What's covered in the NVIDIA-Certified Professional: OpenUSD Development exam, including domain areas, competencies, and exam structure.
How certification validates your expertise and expands your career opportunities in the growing ecosystem of robotics, factory automation, and autonomous vehicles.
Identify study and training resources to prepare for the exam.
Insights to more training offered at GTC to accelerate your skills for developing physical AI such as industrial digital twins and robotics simulation.

Whether you're a seasoned robotics engineer, automation architect, or a 3D developer looking to expand your skills in today's rapidly changing world, this session will empower you to build and validate your OpenUSD skills for the next era of physical AI.
