## Abstract
Scaling mixture-of-experts (MoE) models in production while delivering high performance is challenging. This session explores how the NVIDIA Blackwell NVL72 makes MoE scaling practical and efficient with full stack innovation across compute, networking, and software. Hear from Perplexity AI on why they have adopted MoE models, detailing how they leverage Blackwell NVL72 to deliver real-time search with breakthrough performance.
