## Abstract
Agile Robots is driving a shift in industrial automation—from systems that follow predefined rules to machines that perceive, learn, and act in the physical world through AI. This talk explores how Agile Robots builds on deep experience in industrial automation and combines it with advanced AI to realize physical AI as a new paradigm for designing work, manufacturing, and production, where intelligence is embodied in robotic systems, rather than confined to software. The presentation highlights how Agile Robots is developing today’s and the next generation's robotic systems—including humanoid robots—by integrating NVIDIA Isaac open frameworks and libraries to bring AI-driven flexibility, autonomy, and collaboration to the factory floor, and outlines the technological trajectory ahead.
