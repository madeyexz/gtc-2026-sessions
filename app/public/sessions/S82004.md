## Abstract
As rack power densities surge beyond 100 kilowatts (kW), achieving 100% heat capture becomes increasingly essential for thermal management and operational efficiency. Current coldplate designs focus on high-thermal design power components such as CPUs and GPUs, but future architectures demand complete heat removal across all components. Even at 90% heat capture, a 500kW rack leaves 50kW for air cooling, which is a stretch for even the best air-cooled implementations. Enabling 100% heat capture will be critical for supporting next-generation AI infrastructure. Join us to learn how integrated cooling loops and advanced coldplate technologies enable complete heat removal across the entire server.
