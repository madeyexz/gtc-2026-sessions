## Abstract
In 2024, NVIDIA unveiled nvmath-python, a library designed to bridge the gap between Python scientific community and NVIDIA CUDA-X math libraries, and it’s now generally available. You’ll learn what makes nvmath-python a useful addition for GPU-accelerated computing with Python, along with how to use the library’s unique capabilities to reach extreme levels of performance to solve cutting-edge scientific problems previously unimaginable in Python.
