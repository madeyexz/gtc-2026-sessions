### Petrick Liu (Co-Presenter)
> Petrick joined NVIDIA in 2020. He focuses on developing highly efficient Tensor Core-related operations library.

## Abstract
This ninja-level tutorial will guide you through the techniques to build a low-latency inference kernel for Blackwell using CUDA and PTX. You will learn about techniques like programmatic dependent launch, prologue minimization, lightweight CGA parallelism, and operand swapping in the Blackwell Tensor Cores.
