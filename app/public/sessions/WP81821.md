会议主持人：
Wei Yan, Solution Architect, NVIDIA
Holly Ye, Account Manager, NVIDIA
Join Part 1 of a two-part curated session on how simulation-first design is changing the way healthcare robots are built. You’ll see how teams are using NVIDIA Physical AI—spanning world foundation models, physics-based simulation, synthetic data, and real-time sensor-to-action deployment—to move faster with less risk. This session focuses on how simulation and digital twins are being used in practice to speed up development, test decisions earlier, and optimize systems long before they reach the operating room

How Simulation-First Design Accelerates Healthcare Robotic Development: Learn how teams use digital twins, physics-based environments, and generative simulation to rapidly design, test, and refine robotic systems before they touch the real world.
How World Foundation Models and Synthetic Data Expand Capability and Coverage: See how specialized foundation models and SDG pipelines create rich scenario diversity, improve training efficiency, and strengthen early-stage algorithm performance.
How Developers are Building Next-Generation Devices With NVIDIA Physical AI: Hear from industry leaders using NVIDIA’s simulation and modeling technologies to optimize planning, validate concepts, and shorten product development cycles.
