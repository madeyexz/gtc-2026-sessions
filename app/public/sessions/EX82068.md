## Abstract
This session will explain how KV cache technology improves AI inference performance and scalability. CXL memory can play a transformative role by expanding local and shared infrastructure to save processed results, enabling GPUs to focus on more high-value tasks. Learn how sharing cached work across multiple GPUs not only accelerates response times, but also unlocks new functionalities, driving higher system efficiency and reducing the total cost of inference. Attend this talk to explore the future of AI inference optimization through the benefits of KV cache.
