## Abstract
As organizations integrate AI into production systems, new and complex security challenges are emerging. In this panel discussion, we’ll explore where AI models are most vulnerable—data poisoning, evasion, and prompt-based exploitation—and how adversarial red teaming can help identify and mitigate these risks before attackers do. Learn how research–industry collaboration drives the development of scalable defenses and practical tools for securing machine learning pipelines. By the end of this session, you’ll have a high-level playbook for building resilient AI systems in an evolving threat landscape.
