## Abstract
We are developing noninvasive diagnostic solutions for CAD to replace risk-bearing, high-cost invasive angiography with safe, efficient, and data-rich alternatives. By combining coronary computed tomography angiography (CCTA), AI-based lumen segmentation, and hemodynamics simulation, clinicians can assess both the anatomical and functional significance of coronary lesions — improving patient safety, healthcare efficiency, and clinical precision.
