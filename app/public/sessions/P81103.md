## Abstract
Our goal is to deliver a visually realistic and physically authentic simulation that enables robots to handle daily tasks in ordinary homes (especially deformable objects), and promote large-scale home adoption of robots by bridging the sim2real gap. Challenges include interacting with diverse, non-standard objects and adapting to dynamic environments; expensive and labor-intensive household data collection; the complexity of modeling diverse deformable objects; and the high cost, large size, and expertise of industrial robots.

We contribute diverse high-fidelity assets across four room types via NVIDIA Newton; accurate, fine-grained, and causally interactions with objects through Isaacsim Features; support for multiple robotic embodiments, especially low-cost robots; and efficient and extensive teleoperation methods for data collection with Leader & Follower, Xbox, etc.
