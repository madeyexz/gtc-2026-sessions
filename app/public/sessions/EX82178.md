## Abstract
The convergence of AI and quantum computing is not a distant vision—it’s unfolding now. Generative Quantum AI (GenQAI) is at the forefront of this shift, creating a feedback loop where quantum computers generate novel data that can enhance classical AI models, while AI systems learn from and guide quantum exploration. Quantinuum has been using the NVIDIA CUDA-Q platform to bring these two technologies together. Their convergence unlocks more than technical achievements—it redefines what’s possible. In pharmaceuticals, it makes the acceleration of drug discovery possible by exploring chemical spaces classical computing cannot reach. In materials science, it makes breakthroughs in superconductors and batteries possible that can transform energy and mobility. In finance and logistics, it opens the door to solving optimization problems that define competitiveness.
