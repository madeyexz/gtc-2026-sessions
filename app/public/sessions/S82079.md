## Abstract
Artificial intelligence is driving unprecedented computational demands that rely on massive data access at low latency. Learn how advanced semiconductor technologies—high-bandwidth memory, low-power DRAM, and next-generation storage—have become critical to meeting these computing challenges in AI systems. We will spotlight how Micron’s innovations reduce power consumption in large-scale AI training and inference systems while delivering the bandwidth to advance next-generation generative AI.
