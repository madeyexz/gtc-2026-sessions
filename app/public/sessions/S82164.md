## Abstract
Large-scale, useful quantum computers will require the full machinery of fault tolerance, bringing a formidable challenge for classical control electronics. In this talk, we'll illustrate the importance of hybrid quantum-classical systems by outlining PsiQuantum's single-photon architecture for quantum computing, how it implements quantum error correction, and initial work with NVIDIA to accelerate this with GPU supercomputing.
