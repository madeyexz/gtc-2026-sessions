## Abstract
Scaling next-generation discovery requires more than increased computational power; it also depends on flexible, reliable workload orchestration at large scale. This session examines how Slurm can be used to manage traditional HPC jobs, Kubernetes-based AI workloads with complex topology requirements, and converged environments in a single ecosystem. We will outline the current architecture, recent enhancements, and ongoing community-driven work that target higher efficiency, portability, and interoperability for supercomputing workloads over the coming decade.
