## Abstract
Explore our edge-optimized quantization approach that enables full 8B-scale reasoning on Jetson Orin NX. By combining 1.58-bit and INT4 quantization with custom CUDA kernels and fused attention/Multi-Layer Perceptron (MLP) layers, we achieve 8B inference in just 2 GB VRAM — bringing large-scale generative AI fully offline.
