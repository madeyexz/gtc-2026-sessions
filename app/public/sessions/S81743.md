## Abstract
The next wave of surgical innovation lies in the intelligence behind robotic systems. Advances in accelerated computing—enabling performance gains on the order of thousands—are transforming surgical data into a powerful feedback loop for continuous improvement. This session will share how a massive leap in computational power—up to 10,000 times greater than before—is enabling continuous learning in the operating room. Real-time analytics, AI-generated technique insights, and digital twin simulations are reshaping how surgeons learn, collaborate, and refine their practice. Combined with remote connectivity and scalable compute, these technologies mark a shift toward smarter, more adaptive operating rooms and improved patient outcomes.
