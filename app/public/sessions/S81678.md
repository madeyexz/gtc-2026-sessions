## Abstract
We'll detail our journey, achievements, and insights gained from implementing the Apache Spark RAPIDS accelerator across Snap's most substantial data processing pipelines. A significant cost saving of 76% to 90% has been realized in comparison to utilizing CPU-only jobs. We are currently collaborating with Google and NVIDIA to further deploy the RAPIDS accelerator to a broader range of our production workloads.
