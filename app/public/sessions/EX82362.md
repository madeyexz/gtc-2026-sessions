## Abstract
Capital One leverages its own proprietary, multi-agentic AI framework to help several hundred bank customer service agents that specialize in complex fraud resolution to understand, analyze, respond to real-time customer queries with more speed/quality than ever before. Explore how Capital One uses multi-agent collaboration to process long-form conversational audio between customers and service agents—or across agents—to generate accurate, structured, and low-latency summaries. These summaries enhance the human agent experience—especially by reducing the large cognitive load required to accurately recap long-form discussions, which can last up to an hour for complex fraud scenarios—as well as the customer experience by advancing quality reviews, speed, and agent productivity, while meeting rigorous business standards around accuracy, reliability, and risk management.
