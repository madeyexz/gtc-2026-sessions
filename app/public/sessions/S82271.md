## Abstract
Hyundai Motor Group is advancing factory autonomy by leveraging NVIDIA Omniverse to build scalable, high-fidelity digital twins across its manufacturing operations.

In this session, we share Hyundai’s real-world journey of implementing Omniverse-powered factory digital twins, with a focus on virtual commissioning as a critical enabler for reducing deployment risk, accelerating system integration, and improving factory startup efficiency. Gain insights into how Hyundai virtualizes diverse manufacturing workloads—ranging from robotics and automation systems to production line logic—within a shared, collaborative digital environment. We will also highlight how cross-functional collaboration between engineering, IT, and operations teams is transformed through a unified digital twin platform, enabling faster decision-making and continuous innovation.
