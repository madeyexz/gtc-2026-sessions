## Abstract
This panel brings together leaders to reveal what it truly takes to build a robotics company today. We’ll discuss the integration of real-world data, advanced models, investment, and cloud solutions to accelerate robotics innovation, deployment, and impact on society. Gain insights into the challenges and opportunities of building intelligent, adaptable robots through shared infrastructure, and strategic partnerships, and how open collaboration can make a difference.
