## Abstract
Representing life in silico and designing compounds and therapies that modulate that biology are among the most foundational goals of modern life sciences R&D. The community is at an inflection point: increasing volumes of sophisticated data and foundation modeling efforts require fundamental new technology to accelerate discovery. In this talk, learn about NVIDIA's efforts in addressing these challenges head-on, with new efforts in data generation, data processing, and biological and molecular foundation modeling, and research. We'll cover genomics, multi-omics, foundation model scaling, molecular discovery, and end-to-end AI-scientist workflows in this comprehensive product talk.
