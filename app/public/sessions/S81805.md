## Abstract
The next round of innovation in healthcare is being defined by the fusion of foundation models and intelligent agents, creating systems that learn, reason, and act alongside clinicians to deliver truly personalized medicine. In this session, we explore how AI is transforming the very fabric of healthcare delivery by embedding reasoning systems directly into the flow of care.
 
Agentic AI systems, built on these models, interpret, anticipate, and act—surfacing insights at the right moment, summarizing encounters, and coordinating care across disciplines. The result: care that is faster, safer, and more human.

This talk will show how Epic is using the NVIDIA AI platform in making this future real—combining accelerated computing and specialized medical AI models and agents to enable intelligent, closed-loop healthcare systems.
