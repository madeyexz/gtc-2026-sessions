## Abstract
Inference is where AI systems face real-world constraints: latency users can feel, costs that scale with every request, and reliability requirements that leave no room for failure. In this session, hear lessons in delivering and continuously optimizing inference in production on Together AI, the AI-native cloud—powering Cursor’s Composer 2.0 background coding magenta at massive scale. Drawing on Together’s research-driven inference platform and Cursor’s experience operating AI products under real developer traffic, the discussion explores how foundational systems work translates into real-world improvements in speed, cost, and reliability—serving trillions of tokens per day across multi-data center deployments and running production inference on NVIDIA HGX B200 and NVIDIA GB200 NVL72, even under unpredictable demand.
