## Abstract
The session highlights AUDI’s strategy for integrating AI-driven robotics into both brownfield and greenfield plants, anchored by digital twins of its production facilities. These digital replicas allow AUDI to augment existing industrial robots with advanced AI capabilities, increasing line efficiency, adaptability, and overall competitiveness in the face of rapidly evolving manufacturing technologies. By operating a continuous “digital twin-to-real” loop, AUDI can monitor factory operations in near-real time, simulate changes before deployment, and rapidly adapt to new product variants or process adjustments. NVIDIA’s industrial platforms—such as Omniverse for factory-scale digital twins, Isaac for robotics AI workflows, and NVIDIA’s edge and data center AI systems—provide the compute, simulation, and optimization capabilities making this production model feasible and scalable.
