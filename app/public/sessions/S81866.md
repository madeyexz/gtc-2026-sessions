## Abstract
Pacific Gas and Electric (PG&E), one of the largest utility companies in the United States serving millions across Northern and Central California, is facing the same urgent grid modernization challenge as the broader energy sector—and is meeting it with NVIDIA’s physical AI stack. In this session, Levatas and PG&E will show how Cosmos Reason VLM, Metropolis, Blueprint for Video Search and Summarization (VSS), and synthetic data generation are being leveraged to transform reactive grid maintenance into predictive, automated asset inspection at national scale. 

Learn how PG&E is beginning to deliver rapid, high-accuracy assessments like thermal anomaly detection, segmentation-based corrosion detection, and analog gauge reading at 99% accuracy, while accelerating post-storm recovery and reducing downtime through automated, geo-tagged, time-stamped asset condition reporting.
