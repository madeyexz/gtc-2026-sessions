## Abstract
Learn how NVIDIA’s IT organization engineered an on‑premises AI factory to deliver agentic AI with enterprise‑grade security, reliability, and governance. We’ll walk through the end-to-end technical stack behind NVIDIA’s internal AI platform, from our on-prem enterprise AI factory infrastructure to containerized agentic applications. This includes scalable inference, secure enterprise data access, agent development and evaluation, standardized agent interaction, memory, runtime execution, and data flywheels for continuous improvement. You’ll see how NVIDIA has deployed AI agents for the chip design, IT, supply chain, and developer and employee productivity domains.
