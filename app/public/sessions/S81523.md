## Abstract
This session brings together leading labs to showcase how they are using autonomous AI and high-performance computing to build scalable, reproducible data pipelines from edge to HPC. See real-world demonstrations of agentic AI automating complex, multi-facility workflows, optimizing resources, and accelerating scientific discovery with NVIDIA’s advanced frameworks. The featured labs will share insights on transparent resource management, rapid deployment, and collaborative experimentation that can be reproduced and scaled globally. Learn how these approaches unlock innovative science through seamless orchestration, portability, and reproducibility.
