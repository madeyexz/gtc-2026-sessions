## Abstract
Join Wistron technology leaders to explore how the company’s digital twin platform, built on NVIDIA Omniverse libraries and OpenUSD, is accelerating factory deployment, from Hsinchu to Dallas. Discover how these factory-scale digital twins are enabling many use cases; including streamlined factory design, layout validation, and manufacturing process simulation in the early planning. Get a deep dive into how these digital twins are enabling virtual commissioning and helping identify integration issues among robots, conveyors, and automation systems before physical installation. Learn about the real business outcomes being generated across Wistron’s operations, from faster iteration cycles to fewer deployment risks and eliminated rework during ramp-up.
