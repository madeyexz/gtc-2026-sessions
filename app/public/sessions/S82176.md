## Abstract
As AI infrastructure scales, the traditional “build-then-test” model has become a critical bottleneck. Join NVIDIA and CoreWeave to explore how a shift-left methodology, powered by digital twins, is transforming the AI factory lifecycle. CoreWeave will share how NVIDIA Air decouples software automation from hardware lead times, enabling validation of complex systems well before physical infrastructure arrives. In parallel, NVIDIA uses these digital twin environments to empower a global ecosystem of software partners to develop and optimize next-generation platforms without requiring access to physical hardware. This session demonstrates how a simulation-first approach ensures the entire stack, from cloud automation to ISV solutions, is production-ready on day one, dramatically reducing time-to-market and capital risk.
