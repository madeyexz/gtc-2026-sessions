## Abstract
As one of the world’s most influential animation studios, Pixar has long pushed the boundaries of storytelling through technology. RenderMan has powered Pixar’s films since before Toy Story, evolving with artists, stories and hardware. That evolution continues with Toy Story 5 and the rollout of RenderMan XPU, Pixar’s hybrid CPU+GPU designed for modern production workflows. This session explores how XPU transforms look development, lighting, and final-frame rendering for artists—treating rendering not just as compute, but as an active part of storytelling through faster iteration, visual exploration, and immediate feedback. The talk also highlights Pixar’s Machine Learning Denoiser—used on every Pixar film since Toy Story 4—as a predictable, reliable artist-facing ML tool that accelerates rendering without sacrificing creative control. The session includes live demos of RenderMan XPU running on Dell Precision workstations with NVIDIA GPUs, showing how local GPU power supports performance and creative flow. Attendees will also see the Toy Story 5 trailer rendered entirely with XPU. And don’t miss the end‑of‑session giveaway—one attendee will walk away with a Dell Precision mobile workstation.
