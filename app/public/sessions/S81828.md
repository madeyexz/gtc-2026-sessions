## Abstract
Coupang is evolving from a digital retailer into an AI-native commerce platform by building an AI Factory in partnership with NVIDIA. In this session, we’ll examine how large-scale GPU infrastructure enables end-to-end AI lifecycle management — from data pipelines and model training to real-time inference across billions of interactions. We’ll discuss the system architecture, performance optimizations, and deployment strategies required to scale foundation models across search, recommendation, and merchant services.
