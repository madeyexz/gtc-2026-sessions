## Abstract
This work investigates communication dynamics in distributed LLM serving—analyzing how various parallelization approaches coordinate data exchange between GPU workers during inference. We study transformer-based models as representative examples of contemporary architectures widely used in operational deployments. Our work combines detailed profiling measurements with predictive analytical models to characterize communication behavior across different parallelization configurations.
