## Abstract
The decisions AI-first companies make on Day Zero will radically shift based on initial resources. Does a $5 million seed round change the fundamental AI strategy compared to a bootstrapped $0 start? Absolutely.
This session explores four distinct Day Zero scenarios, each representing a different starting capital: $0 (Bootstrapped), $500,000 (Pre-Seed), $5 million (Seed/Series A), and $50 million (Series B+). For each scenario, we'll highlight high-leverage AI-first tactics, illustrating how capital immediately dictates where to spend resources and how fast to build proprietary technology. You'll leave with a clear, resource-aligned playbook for your own AI-first launch.
