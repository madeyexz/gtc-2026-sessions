## Abstract
Explore new techniques for accelerating GPU-aware MPI Allreduce using direct send/receive communication with throttling. Our design improves scalability and overlaps computation with communication, achieving up to 7% faster nanoGPT training while maintaining strong performance across diverse workloads.
