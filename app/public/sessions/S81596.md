## Abstract
Decart’s Lucy Restyle is a World Transformation Model that reimagines any video, game, or camera feed into a new digital world in real time. We will dive into how Decart and NVIDIA optimized Lucy Restyle inference for low-latency, cost-efficient cloud deployment on NVIDIA B40 GPUs through GeForce NOW (GFN). Finally, we’ll demonstrate how to harness Lucy Restyle to Lucy Restyle completely restyle titles via the ultimate modding platform: RTX Remix.
