# AI Solves GPS Outages: Making Vehicle Navigation Robust

`Poster` `Technical - Intermediate` `Robotics`

## Speakers
### KUANG HAO LIU (Author)
> Kuang-Hao Liu is a professor and the director of the Institute of Communications Engineering at National Tsing Hua University, Taiwan. His recent research focuses on wireless energy harvesting, millimeter-wave communications, and intelligent radio access technologies.

## Abstract
Kalman filters (KF) are the most successful tracking algorithm for vehicle navigation. Its accuracy drops when the process noise covariance matrix Q is fixed. Conventional approaches for updating the Qmatrix have stability issues due to aggressive updates. We propose a reinforcement learning (RL)-guided adaptive Q-update for robust vehicle navigation. The proposed RL-guided KF achieves stable state estimation under GPS loss and tough terrain.
