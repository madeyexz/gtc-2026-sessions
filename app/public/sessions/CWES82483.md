## Abstract
Modern AI workloads live or die by the performance of a handful of critical GPU kernels. In this Connect With the Experts session, there are no slides—just open, in-depth discussions with NVIDIA engineers about FlashInfer, cuDNN, and the optimized kernels that power today’s models. Bring your hardest performance questions, unusual model shapes, and kernel-level challenges to talk through how choices around attention, Tensor operations, memory access patterns, fusion, and precision affect behavior on current GPU architectures. The conversation will center on your real-world scenarios: what limits you’re hitting, what you’ve already tried, and which directions are most promising to explore next. You’ll walk away with concrete ideas and heuristics you can apply later with your own tools and workflows, plus pointers to resources for going deeper.

Important: Connect With the Experts sessions are interactive sessions that give you a unique opportunity to meet, in either a group or one-on-one setting, with the minds behind NVIDIA’s products and research to get your questions answered. Attendees can meet with experts on a first-come, first-served basis.
