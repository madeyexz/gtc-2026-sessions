## Abstract
NVFP4 and MXFP8 are the new numerical formats in Blackwell to significantly speed up LLM training and inference versus previous formats based on FP8, BF16, or FP16. In this session, we’ll discuss how to achieve this speedup without compromising the stability of the training and model accuracy. We’ll bring experts with deep knowledge on both the hardware and the software side, so that you learn how to enable NVFP4 and MXFP8 on your GPU to reduce memory footprint and accelerate training and inference.

Important: Connect With the Experts sessions are interactive sessions that give you a unique opportunity to meet, in either a group or one-on-one setting, with the minds behind NVIDIA’s products and research to get your questions answered. Attendees can meet with experts on a first-come, first-served basis.
