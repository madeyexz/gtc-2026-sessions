## Abstract
Join us for an interactive Connect with Experts session focused on the NVCC CUDA Compiler Toolchain—the core engine behind CUDA C++ language support, compiler functionality, and performance optimization on NVIDIA platforms.

NVCC compiler experts will be available to discuss topics including CUDA language features, compilation workflows, optimization strategies, and performance tuning considerations across different workloads and architectures. Bring real-world questions, challenges, or areas of curiosity: from understanding compilation decisions and generated code to navigating advanced optimization and debugging scenarios.
