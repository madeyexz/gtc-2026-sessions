## Abstract
The automotive industry is undergoing a major shift as AI, simulation, and digital twins redefine how vehicles are designed, engineered, manufactured, and validated. As automakers move to AI-enabled, data-driven development pipelines, they’re unlocking new levels of efficiency, quality, and innovation across the vehicle life cycle.

In this panel, leaders from NVIDIA, Hyundai, Lucid, and Schaeffler will explore how industrial AI, virtual development environments, large-scale digital twins, and advanced simulation are accelerating automotive innovation. They’ll share real examples of AI-powered design, automated engineering, factory optimization, and robotics training converging into a unified end-to-end pipeline, along with insights on the technologies and collaboration driving industry transformation.
