## Abstract
AI in healthcare is moving beyond perception and prediction toward real-time action in the physical world. Nowhere is this shift more demanding than the operating room, where conditions change continuously and coordination across systems is critical.

This session introduces the agentic operating room—a physical AI architecture where distributed agents across imaging, robotics, sensors, and clinical applications run locally, reason independently, and synchronize through shared context and orchestration. See how NVIDIA’s physical AI stack and partner network enables this workflow, from simulation and digital twins to accelerated training and low-latency edge inference.

Join Pertias.ai to explore how physical AI is turning the operating room into a coordinated, software-defined system—unlocking safer, more adaptive, and more autonomous surgical workflows.
