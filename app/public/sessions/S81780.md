## Abstract
Learn how low-cost and high throughput next-generation sequencing from Ultima, combined with NVIDIA’s accelerated computing technologies, is shaping the future of genomics. Discover how these innovations enable research at an unprecedented scale across academic data generation, drug discovery, and diagnostics. We will discuss how NVIDIA’s accelerated computing technologies power Ultima’s roadmap and latest technology advancements, opening new applications areas like virtual cell modeling, population scale genomics, and cancer detection.
