## Abstract
AI‑native 6G is a strategic opportunity for the United States to lead the next era of wireless infrastructure, not just adopt it. Building on the AI-WIN initiative and the first all‑American AI‑native wireless stack announced in 2025, this session focuses on moving from concepts and demos to deployable systems. Speakers from government, industry, and the broader ecosystem will explain why AI‑native 6G matters for U.S. competitiveness and security, how policy and public‑private partnerships accelerate progress, and why an implementation‑first approach must advance with standards. The session will cover deployment timelines, key milestones, progress on AI-RAN for 5G and 6G using the NVIDIA AI Aerial platform, and early lessons from AI‑native stack trials, including pre‑6G applications and innovations toward commercial‑grade AI‑native 6G networks.
