## Abstract
Criteo operates a real-time bidding and recommendation platform that processes over 500 billion requests per day under a strict 20ms latency budget, powered by dozens of production models. 

This session presents our transition from CPU-based, “classical” machine learning to GPU-accelerated Deep Tabular architectures designed as modular, drop-in replacements, enabling deep learning transition without a full platform rewrite.  We will share key architectural choices and dive into inference optimizations required to meet extreme throughput and latency constraints, including client-side batching, Tensor fusion, and resolving bottlenecks in Triton dynamic batching and serving layers. Learn how this transition delivered a 5% business uplift and established the technical foundation for transformer-based, sequence-driven commerce modeling.
