# AI-Driven Industrial Safety Monitoring: Real-Time Detection and Physics-Informed Safety Risk Reasoning

`Poster` `Technical - Beginner` `Computer Vision / Video Analytics`

## Speakers
### Sangho Yoon (Author)
> Sangho Yoon is an AI researcher at Guardian AI and holds a master's degree in artificial intelligence. His research interests are reinforcement learning and autonomous driving, and he is currently involved in developing industrial safety systems and robots using computer vision.

## Abstract
We present an AI-based industrial safety monitoring system that performs real-time personal protective equipment (PPE) detection and worker action recognition. Using physics-informed risk reasoning, the system generates automated safety summaries and actionable recommendations, supporting evidence-based decision-making. Deployed on server and edge platforms, it significantly reduces PPE noncompliance and provides timely insights for operators and managers in complex industrial environments.
