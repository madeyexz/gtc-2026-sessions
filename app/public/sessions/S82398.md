## Abstract
Semiconductor teams face exploding design complexity, shrinking windows, and increasing verification challenges. This session shows how the Microsoft Discovery AI for Science platform, combined with Synopsys Agent Engineers, introduces an agentic approach to electronic design automation (EDA) that automates routine steps, accelerates expert decision‑making, and keeps data secure on Azure. Discovery’s Science Copilot orchestrates multi‑agent plans leveraging Azure AI resources and a graph‑grounded knowledge layer, while Synopsys agents drive proven flows levering domain expertise—spanning spec‑to‑RTL, simulation, timing, and sign‑off—directly on Azure HPC infrastructure. See how novel methodology integrates with existing Synopsys tools, and how NVIDIA‑accelerated Azure AI infrastructure results in faster time‑to‑PPA, fewer iterations, and more predictable schedules.
