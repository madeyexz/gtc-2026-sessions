## Abstract
Explore how AI and computer vision can read sound visually. This project presents a digital turntable that plays vinyl records using only a 4K camera and deep learning to reconstruct audio from microscopic groove images in real time. Using NVIDIA GPU acceleration, we merge art and technology to preserve fragile analog media and reveal new possibilities for sound restoration, cultural heritage, and multi-modal AI — where image and sound become one expressive language.
