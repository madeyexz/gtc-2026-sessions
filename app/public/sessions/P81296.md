## Abstract
Multi-modal data from diverse domains is the lifeblood of the Gen AI revolution, but curating and discovering datasets is expensive and time consuming. With the scarcity of efficient tools to unify data discovery, debiasing, and blending multi-modal human-labeled datasets for reuse in dynamic downstream model development tasks, there is a tremendous opportunity to drastically reduce data annotation cost and data pre-processing compute.

We present inventions utilizing technologies such as agentic AI, synthetic data generation, and multi-modal embedding models closely integrated with the NVIDIA tool suite. Our end-to-end ecosystem exhibits downstream performance improvements on multiple task types (pre-training, post-training). These tools can rapidly cut down cycle times for foundation model teams, and power smarter and more aligned Gen AI models across numerous industries.
