## Abstract
For AI and data center growth, time-to-power is most critical bottleneck. In the United States, average time for interconnection is between three to five years. This panel with Southwest Power Pool, GE Vernova, Southern California Edison, and ERCOT will discuss how they are using agentic AI and digital twins to better understand existing capacity, peak loads, and accelerating the interconnection process.
