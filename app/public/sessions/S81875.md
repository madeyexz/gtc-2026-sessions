# Interactive Digital Twin for Fusion With Integrated Sensors and Agentic AI

`Talk` `Technical - Beginner` `Simulation / Modeling / Design`

## Schedule
- **Thursday, 2026-03-19** 12:00 PM - 12:40 PM @ SJCC LL20AB (LL)

## Speakers
### Raffi Nazikian (Presenter)
> Raffi M. Nazikian is a physicist known for his contributions to nuclear fusion research and plasma physics. He has been associated with the Princeton Plasma Physics Laboratory and has conducted significant work at the DIII-D National Fusion Facility.

## Abstract
The session will describe the development of the digital twin for the DIII-D Experimental Fusion Reactor. The talk will describe the development of the AI models and live sensor data integrated with the CAD data to develop an interactive digital twin.
