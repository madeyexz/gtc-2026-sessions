## Abstract
Generative AI is rapidly becoming the defining workload of modern computing, but securing these powerful systems without compromising performance is still a challenge for most enterprises. This session dives into how you can lock down your most valuable AI assets—models, data, and prompts—while continuing to push the limits on speed, scale and insights.

Google and NVIDIA will share how confidential computing on NVIDIA GPUs can protect your data and model using accelerated computing, so your information stays shielded even while in use.
