## Abstract
Scaling complex models requires infrastructure that evolves with your code. From prototyping to deployment, learn how to optimize your NVIDIA infrastructure for training and inference workloads at scale. Join Erwan Menard, SVP of Product, for a technical look into Crusoe Cloud’s high-performance, energy-first platform and Dhruv Batra, Co-founder and Chief Scientist at Yutori, to hear how they are leveraging Crusoe’s Managed Inference service to re-imagine how people interact on the web

In this session, we will deep-dive into Crusoe’s latest capabilities, optimized for the newest NVIDIA GPU architectures. You will learn how Crusoe Cloud will help you navigate the technical demands of massive-scale model training, efficient fine-tuning, and low-latency / high throughput inference. Dhruv will join Erwan to share how Yutori brought their own model to Crusoe’s Managed Inference Service to run agents at scale and they will discuss how to optimize multi-architecture inference for the agentic era. Whether you are an ML engineer optimizing compute resources, a leader scaling production workloads, or a developer delivering the next AI-powered agent or application, this session will show you how to unlock the full potential of NVIDIA compute on Crusoe Cloud.
