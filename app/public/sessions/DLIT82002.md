## Abstract
In this hands-on lab you'll learn, as an AI factory administrator, how real workloads are run on a cluster. Intended for IT types (not data scientists!), this lab will have you configuring Run:ai, allocating and monitoring resources, and building a workload with NVIDIA NIMS to understand just what the user experience is, and what tools are at your disposal to make the AI factory workloads actually work.
 
Prerequisite(s):

To be successful, participants should have an understanding of AI clusters, Linux, and containers.
Bring a laptop as this session is very interactive!
