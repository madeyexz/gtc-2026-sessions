## Abstract
Building physically accurate digital twins for electrical grid infrastructure is becoming critical to be able simulate and predict outages during extreme weather events. We'll discuss Southern California Edison's journey of adopting Omniverse as a platform to bring all its ecosystem partners to build digital twins of the grid. Presenters will showcase how SoftServe built the solution, demonstrating Southern California Edison's vision, and in parallel helped Siemens Energy build scalable product integrating physics and AI-based models from substation.
