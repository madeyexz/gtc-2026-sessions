In this 60-minute wide-ranging discussion, NVIDIA Chief Scientist and GPU architect Bill Dally engages in a focused dialogue with Google's Chief Scientist Jeff Dean, co-instigator of TPUs, overall Gemini co-tech lead, and pioneer in large-scale ML systems.
