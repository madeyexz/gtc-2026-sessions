## Abstract
Your AI factory is ready for action—now it’s time to put it to work.

In this session, two NVIDIA senior solutions architects will walk you through the essential steps for deploying and managing your AI infrastructure. Aiming at teams beginning their AI factory journey, we'll focus on real-world deployment practices and insights drawn from leading customer environments. Together, we’ll discuss how to streamline cluster onboarding, configuration, and monitoring, laying the foundation for efficient and resilient operations from Day One.
