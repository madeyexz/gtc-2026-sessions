## Abstract
Retail is no longer a choice between online and in-store. Success now depends on physical and digital experiences operating as one. Instacart is reinventing retail with AI by connecting stores, shelves, customers, and digital experiences, from edge to cloud. This session explores how Instacart applies AI across the retail stack to build real-time understanding of physical stores—one of grocery's most valuable and least digitized assets. Instacart offers enterprise solutions designed to democratize AI for grocers of all sizes, including Kroger and Sprouts. Powered by NVIDIA Jetson at the edge and NVIDIA Dynamo-Triton in the cloud, signals are processed on GPUs to continuously build and update a real-time digital model of in-store reality and inventory.
