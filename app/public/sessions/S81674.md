## Abstract
The pharmaceutical industry’s quest for self-driving labs demands convergence of intelligent AI agents and precision robotics to create biology data factories and new medicine foundries. Multiply Labs provides the physical execution layer, using modular robotic clusters and digital twins to automate complex cell therapy workflows, reducing variability and setup time while capturing high-quality data. Thermo Fisher is delivering the lab of the future with industry-leading instruments, automation, and digital platforms that enable fully connected, AI-ready labs and end-to-end automated workflows. HighRes Biosolutions powers the labs of top TechBio and pharma through advanced automation, autonomous mobile robots, and agentic orchestration. This connected approach is the blueprint for truly self-driving bio and manufacturing labs.
