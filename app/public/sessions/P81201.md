## Abstract
Our system integrates neural networks with SVM-based feature extraction to automatically detect liver regions in MRI-PDFF images while minimizing false positives. By employing whole-liver quantification instead of traditional ROI sampling, it objectively computes mean PDFF values for more accurate and consistent fatty liver assessment and follow-up.
