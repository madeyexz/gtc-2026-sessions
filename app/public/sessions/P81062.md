# AI-Orchestrating Platform for Drug Repurposing

`Poster` `General Interest` `Simulation / Modeling / Design`

## Speakers
### YIN-HSONG HSU (Author)
> Yin-Hsong Hsu is a pioneer in Taiwan’s healthcare technology, with 20 years of experience bridging innovation and medicine. He is the president of Repurgenesis, a company specializing in AI-driven drug repurposing, and a visiting professor at the AI School of National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University...

## Abstract
We'll introduce a multi-model AI-orchestrated platform that integrates structure-based screening, knowledge-graph reasoning, and gene-expression analysis to accelerate systematic drug repurposing. By unifying multi-modal data and diverse AI models, the system enables cross-domain reasoning from molecular binding prediction to disease-gene inference. A case study on Bremelanotide illustrates how this approach identifies new therapeutic indications through end-to-end computational orchestration.
