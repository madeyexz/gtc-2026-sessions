## Abstract
Join Undersecretary Dario Gil for a forward-looking update on the Genesis Mission—a bold initiative redefining how science, technology, and global collaboration drive scientific discovery. This session will showcase early successes of NVIDIA’s long-standing collaborations with the U.S. Department of Energy and ongoing work with leading research centers such as the Argonne, Los Alamos, and Oak Ridge national laboratories. Discover how these collaborations are advancing breakthroughs across energy, quantum technology, and materials science—and learn how developers, researchers, and business leaders alike can help shape the next frontier of scientific innovation.
