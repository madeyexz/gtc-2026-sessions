## Abstract
To solve the slow, manual tuning and costly physical trials hindering robotic grinding, we present HolonCloud: a physics-based digital twin powered by NVIDIA Isaac Sim. This framework leverages synthetic data and material simulation to accurately predict results, eliminating physical validation and enabling an autonomous, reinforcement learning-driven sim-to-real workflow.
