## Abstract
In this session, SAP shares how it partners with NVIDIA to accelerate enterprise AI from model development to production-grade agentic workflows. This session shows how SAP combines NVIDIA AI with deep business context from SAP applications to move AI beyond prototypes into real-world impact. Learn how developers build and adapt domain-specific models, operationalize them using SAP GenAI hub and NVIDIA NIM, and apply them through SAP Joule for Developers to tackle complex challenges like code explanation and cloud migration. The session closes with a forward-looking view on agentic and physical AI, where live business context connects digital intelligence to real-world execution.
