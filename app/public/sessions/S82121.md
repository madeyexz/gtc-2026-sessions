## Abstract
A new era of storytelling is unfolding, where artists and intelligent systems co-create, challenge tradition, and push the boundaries of expression. This panel brings together leaders at the intersection of art, education, and technology, including the creative visionaries shaping the next frontier of cinema, design, and digital experience.  From generative animation tools built for Hollywood to AI frameworks informing visual storytelling, these panelists are not asking whether AI belongs in the creative process but how to wield it with intention, imagination, and integrity. Together, they will explore what authorship means when machines become part of the medium, how education is evolving to prepare tomorrow's creators, and why protecting creative agency matters more than ever in an AI-powered world.  This is a conversation about design and disruption, culture and code, where the future of art is not automated but amplified.
