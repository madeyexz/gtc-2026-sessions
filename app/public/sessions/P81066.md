## Abstract
We developed an advanced intraoperative lymph node diagnostic framework that integrates rapid fresh pathology imaging with an AI-assisted analytical pipeline. The demonstrated approach enables accurate intraoperative diagnosis of lymph nodes, markedly improving diagnostic sensitivity and reducing the false-negative rate of intraoperative frozen section analysis.
