## Abstract
Manufacturers have more operational data than ever. When something goes wrong on the line, data alone still only tell us what happened, not why. In this session, Tulip shares how Factory Playback, powered by NVIDIA Blueprint for video search and summarization, creates a new standard for operational truth by fusing synchronized multi-camera video with real-time manufacturing data.

Learn how Tulip has deployed solutions with Terex, a global manufacturer of lifting and material processing equipment. We’ll cover Factory Playback solutions for creating a searchable "time machine" across production and warehouse operations and associated ROI. By synchronizing operational events with multi-camera video, data, and workflows, teams gain verifiable visual context to enforce standard operating procedures (SOPs), accelerate root-cause analysis, and automate responses to process deviations.
