## Abstract
Join a special presentation from our 2025-26 Graduate Fellowship recipients to learn "what's next" from the world of research and academia. Sponsored projects involve a variety of technical challenges, including machine learning, humanoid robotics, computer vision, speech and audio, reliability, drug discovery, agentic AI and trustworthy AI. These minds lead the future in our industry, and we're proud to support the 2025-26 NVIDIA Graduate Fellows. We'll also announce the 2026-27 Graduate Fellows at this session.
