### Magnus Strengert (Presenter)
> Magnus Strengert is the engineering manager of the Nsight Compute team at NVIDIA. Magnus obtained his Ph.D. in computer science from the University of Stuttgart, Germany in 2010. He joined NVIDIA in 2008.

## Abstract
Explore practical techniques for maximizing Tensor Core performance in your CUDA kernels. We’ll discuss CUTLASS-based coding patterns to unlock efficient pipeline utilization and streamlined data management. Together, we’ll use Nsight Compute to analyze, optimize, and fine-tune your workflows for optimal Tensor Core performance.
