## Abstract
We accelerated JAK3 inhibitor discovery from months to hours using NVIDIA NIM microservices with GenMol and DiffDock on RTX Ada 6000 GPUs. Our pipeline identified novel drug candidates with better binding than FDA-approved treatments. This end-to-end workflow runs on a single GPU and combines NIM-accelerated AI generation with physics-based validation. Pharmaceutical companies and academic researchers can deploy this modular NIM-powered framework to dramatically reduce drug development timelines and costs for any therapeutic target.
