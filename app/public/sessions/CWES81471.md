This session will be an extension to the session led by NVIDIA senior solutions architect Kristof De Brouwer, titled "Mastering AI Infrastructure At Scale — a DevOps Playbook." You can ask questions about your scaled AI deployments of our technical experts, explore deployment strategies in depth, and engage in discussions on optimizing NVIDIA AI infrastructure for your specific environments.
