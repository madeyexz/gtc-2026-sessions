## Abstract
We present a light field-based 3D planning platform that enhances visualization, precision, and workflow efficiency in radiation therapy planning. The system integrates advanced eye-tracking, light-field rendering, and GPU-accelerated computation to provide real-time, glasses-free 3D visualization of anatomical structures and radiation dose distributions. By performing automated image segmentation and continuous ray-tracing calculations, the platform enables clinicians to instantly identify underdosed or overdosed regions and make rapid adjustments. Clinical trials demonstrate significant improvements in both accuracy and efficiency, reducing the experience-based planning gap from 67 minutes to 16 minutes. Integrating powerful GPU computing not only ensures real-time performance, but also establishes a new standard for intelligent, interactive, and precise radiation therapy planning.
