## Abstract
Whether it was the transition from black-and-white to color or from linear to streaming, the entertainment industry is well-versed in innovation, transformation, and disruption. As content produced by AI models becomes more widespread, media companies face a unique set of issues as the industry works to protect its intellectual property and maintain the trust of its creatives and audiences. In a fireside chat with Paramount’s Chief Product Officer Dane Glasgow, we will hear how he and his company leverage new and emerging technologies in support of their employees and the creative community, while taking definitive measures to protect its IP and storytelling.
