# Accelerated Quantum Supercomputing: Build a Multi-GPU, Multi-QPU, Multi-User Environment for Hybrid Algorithms

`Poster` `Technical - Intermediate` `Simulation / Modeling / Design`

## Speakers
### James Fletcher (Author)
> As head of solutions architecture for ORCA Computing, James works with customers and partners to break new ground on how photonic quantum processors can integrate with high performance compute, especially for AI/ML workloads in science and industrial research.

## Abstract
Quantum computers can run some calculations faster than classical computers, but integration with classical computing is required. Our work is the first-ever demonstration of a multi-QPU and multi-GPU environment fully integrated into a data center.
