# Live Agent Gauntlet

`Public Special Event` `General Interest`

## Schedule
- **Wednesday, 2026-03-18** 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM @ GTC Park Stage

## Abstract
Put your AI agent to the test in a competitive, benchmark-driven challenge. In this esports-style hackathon, teams are given the challenge in advance and have dedicated time to build and optimize their agents before the event.

On the day of the competition, teams will load their agents live and evaluate performance head-to-head on standardized benchmarks. Results will be tracked in real time on a public leaderboard, showcasing the strongest approaches and optimizations.
