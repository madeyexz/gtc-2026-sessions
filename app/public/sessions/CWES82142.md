## Abstract
Designing gigawatt-scale AI factories requires a shift from bespoke construction to standardized, modular architecture. This session introduces DSX, NVIDIA’s modular reference design for large-scale AI infrastructure. We move beyond traditional data center design to a purpose-built framework optimized for the latest liquid-cooled compute.
