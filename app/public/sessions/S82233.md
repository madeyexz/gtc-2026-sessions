## Abstract
Physical AI represents a fundamental shift in computing, demanding the seamless integration of three distinct workloads—high-fidelity simulation, large-scale model training, and low-latency real-world inference—which NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang calls the "Three Computer" problem. Join this panel featuring leaders from Nebius and pioneering robotics and simulation companies as they dissect what it takes to bring this architecture to life. Moving beyond theory, the discussion will focus on the concrete infrastructure, orchestration layers (including NVIDIA OSMO and Cosmos), and pipeline strategies required to close the loop between virtual worlds and physical reality.
