## Abstract
Explore a Multi-Tool ReAct AI Agent that integrates XGBoost, GPU-accelerated SHAP, and LLM reasoning on NVIDIA NIM to preserve and scale tacit manufacturing expertise. We demonstrate how this agentic AI framework improves optimization, interpretability, and operational decision-making, helping manufacturers accelerate digital transformation through explainable, data-driven intelligence.
