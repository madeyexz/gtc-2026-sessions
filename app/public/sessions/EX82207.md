## Speakers
### Amritanshu Jain (Presenter)
> Amritanshu Jain is a co-founder at Simplismart. He previously worked at Oracle Cloud and Capillary. Amritanshu has been breathing machine learning and building AI stacks for enterprises to enable inference at scale for more than seven years.

## Abstract
One size doesn’t fit all for inference, and black-box APIs break down when real workloads demand clear service-level agreements (SLAs) around latency, cost, reliability, and output quality. This session will walk you step by step through how you can choose the right inference stack for your use case, build it yourself, and operate it in production, using practical techniques to balance performance trade-offs across real-time workloads.
