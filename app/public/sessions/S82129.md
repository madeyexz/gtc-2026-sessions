## Abstract
Accelerating AI at enterprise scale demands a unified platform that seamlessly connects data, software, and infrastructure. This session examines how IBM and NVIDIA bring together GPU-proximate storage, open AI and data services, and accelerated computing to deliver production-ready AI at scale. Learn how IBM Fusion and watsonx integrate with NVIDIA’s platform to eliminate data bottlenecks, streamline pipelines, and enable secure, scalable deployments. We’ll also highlight how IBM Storage powered NVIDIA’s Velox project—advancing a new era of GPU-aware, data-intelligent infrastructure.

Also hear from Liqiang Wang, BioHPC Director at UT Southwestern, on how the medical center is deploying next-generation AI infrastructure with IBM Fusion and NVIDIA’s AI Data Platform to train larger models, process complex datasets, and power advanced research - from accelerated drug discovery to AI-driven clinical training and improved researcher productivity.
