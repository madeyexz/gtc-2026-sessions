## Abstract
AI cloud providers are under pressure to deploy next‑generation AI infrastructure faster, more efficiently, and at multi‑gigawatt scale. In this session, Lenovo introduces the AI Cloud Gigafactory program, co-developed with NVIDIA to industrialize hyperscale AI cloud buildouts. See how this framework, with Lenovo's global manufacturing, integrated solutions and services, enables rapid, energy-efficient deployment of NVIDIA Blackwell Ultra (GB300) systems and provides a seamless path to the next-generation Vera Rubin architecture. Leveraging real-world AI cloud deployments with NScale, this session demonstrates how Lenovo’s Neptune liquid cooling has evolved from niche HPC roots into a scalable foundation for multi-gigawatt AI clouds, utilizing global manufacturing and end-to-end services to accelerate time-to-first-token and de-risk the industry’s shift to liquid-cooled infrastructure.
