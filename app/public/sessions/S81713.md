## Abstract
Autonomous vehicles (AVs) are emerging as the first mass-deployed physical AI agents, reshaping mobility and driving new demands in AI, safety, and scalable development. This special address explores the industry’s path to Level 4 autonomy and how NVIDIA is helping accelerate it. With the DRIVE Hyperion architecture, manufacturers (OEMs) and AV developers can build scalable, safety-certified autonomy solutions. NVIDIA will present its cloud-to-car platform—spanning training, simulation, and in-vehicle compute—to support developing and validating next-generation drive-policy models. The session will also touch on how agentic AI is poised to transform AVs end to end, from automating data-driven development and validation to enabling more intelligent, adaptive in-car experiences. Gain insights into L4 software architectures, the DRIVE Hyperion platform, and ecosystem collaboration across OEMs, AV developers, mobility providers, and robotaxi companies.
