## Abstract
AI is rapidly transforming the cybersecurity landscape, with security operations centers (SOCs) facing growing AI-powered threats that overwhelm traditional tools and workflows. AI-driven defense is therefore no longer optional—it is a necessity. Organizations are not only re-architecting their SOCs but also their infrastructure, with the transition to AI factories. 


This session explores how organizations can move from experimentation to secure deployment of AI agents using an agentic security platform that streamlines analyst workflows and orchestrates adaptive defenses across dynamic environments. Learn how CrowdStrike’s agentic cybersecurity platform integrates with NVIDIA’s AI stack to secure AI across the enterprise, building a resilient foundation for AI-driven cyber defense at enterprise scale.
