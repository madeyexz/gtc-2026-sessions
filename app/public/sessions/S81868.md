## Abstract
Learn the basics of quantum error correction (QEC) and why decoding is a primary bottleneck for quantum computing. Using hands-on exercises, learn about key decoder metrics like accuracy, scalability, throughput, and latency and their implications for quantum computation. Lastly, implement your own AI decoder workflow using the CUDA-Q QEC library. We assume that you're familiar with the fundamentals of quantum computing (gates, circuits, measurement, etc.), but do not necessarily have experience with QEC.
