## Abstract
Manufacturing is advancing automation, yet factories still depend on human input for planning, quality, and operations. The next step is autonomous, self-optimizing factories powered by industrial AI.

L&T Technology Services (LTTS) introduces its Lights-Out Factory (LoF) solution, built on NVIDIA’s Industrial AI platform, Omniverse, Isaac, and CUDA-accelerated AI. LoF integrates digital twins, robotics, and AI-driven decision-making into a closed-loop system, enabling factories to move from assisted automation to autonomy.

This session shows how LTTS combines manufacturing expertise with NVIDIA’s AI to create learning factories. Discover how virtual twins simulate workflows, AI adapts to real-time conditions, and robotics improve yield, safety, and throughput. Real-world results include reduced cycle times, improved yields, and faster ramp-ups, with a roadmap for scaling LoF deployments
