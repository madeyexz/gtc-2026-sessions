# From Kernels to Clusters: How PyTorch Powers High-Performance AI (Presented by Meta)

## Speakers
### Alban Desmaison (Presenter)
> Alban Desmaison has been working on PyTorch since nearly the beginning, first during his Ph.D. at the University of Oxford and now at Meta. He's focused on maintaining core components, designing a wide breadth of features and fostering the PyTorch community.

## Abstract
Discover how PyTorch is redefining high-performance AI from the ground up. This session explores PyTorch’s latest advances—from custom kernel authoring and the Helion compiler stack to Monarch’s next-generation distributed runtime for reinforcement learning to Torchcomms’ vendor-neutral, scalable communication layer. We’ll highlight how PyTorch’s robust fault tolerance and elasticity enable reliable training at massive scale, and how these innovations together create a unified, production-ready stack for both research and deployment. Learn why PyTorch is the open foundation for AI innovation across clusters, clouds, and devices.
