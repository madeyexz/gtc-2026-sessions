## Abstract
Capital Group Investor Onboarding Transformation:

Infosys modernized and deployed a portfolio guidelines analysis solution on Infosys AI Next platform, powered by NVIDIA NIM for Capital Group. This AI-based solution streamlines the review of compliance language to speed up client onboarding, enable analysis and research, and reduce reputational and financial risk.

The solution automatically extracts compliance language from investment guidelines documents, categorizes it, and maps the language to applicable compliance rules. This implementation reduces manual work and enables portfolio guidelines team to review and approve onboarding of clients with significantly higher speed and precision.

The platform, in production today, has increased the efficiency of review and processing volume significantly across customers’ onboarding workflows.
