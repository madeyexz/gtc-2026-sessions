## Abstract
Interpreting volumetric CT scans using vision-language models requires significant GPU resources and robust spatial focus. GLARE introduces slice-wise visual-instruction prompting, overlaying explicit blue contours generated by NVIDIA VISTA-3D on every axial CT slice to direct model attention to clinically relevant regions. Combined with a compact Qwen-2.5 decoder, this design enables high-performance medical reasoning with reduced VRAM footprint and inference time, delivering state-of-the-art VQA accuracy on medical benchmarks.
