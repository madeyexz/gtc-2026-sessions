## Abstract
Our poster demonstrates new solutions for robust, accelerated dementia classification by training a 3D ResNet34 model on U.S. Food and Drug Administration-cleared SubtleHD enhanced MR images, and explores how to improve performance by accelerating the ConvNeXt enhancement pipeline using NVIDIA TensorRT on various GPUs. This work is timely because it provides a validated, high-efficiency pipeline that resolves the critical trade-off between rapid MR scanning and reliable AI-guided diagnosis in neurology.
