## Speakers
### Aaron Luk (Presenter)
> Aaron Luk leads product management for the Universal Scene Description (OpenUSD) ecosystem across NVIDIA Omniverse. Previously, Aaron co-developed and deployed OpenUSD into production pipelines at Pixar.

## Abstract
Universal Scene Description (OpenUSD) is the foundational 3D data framework of NVIDIA’s physical AI technology stack, serving as a standard across industries for building digital twins and AI in 3D worlds. As the underlying technology for NVIDIA Omniverse libraries, Isaac Sim, and SimReady assets, OpenUSD streamlines development pipelines, enhances data compatibility across 3D tools, and establishes standardized processes for complex physical AI workflows. Join this session to learn about the latest developments and next major milestones of OpenUSD, and how OpenClaw can automate the continual assembly, validation, and synchronization of large-scale USD scenes and USD standards implementation across simulation and production environments.
