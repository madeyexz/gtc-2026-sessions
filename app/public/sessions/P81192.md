## Abstract
Our goal is to develop emotion-aware voice agents capable of understanding and responding with appropriate emotion. Traditional agents, relying solely on text-based sentiment analysis, miss critical emotional cues conveyed through speech. Our research integrates multi-modal sentiment analysis, processing both the semantic content (text) and the prosodic features (speech) to create a holistic emotional understanding. This allows for more natural, empathetic, and engaging human-computer interactions.
