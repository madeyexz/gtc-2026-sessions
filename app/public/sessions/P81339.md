## Abstract
We demonstrate Iterative quantum phase estimation (QPE) simulations for quantum chemistry up to 31 qubits on 64 GPUs using chemqulacs-gpu, our distributed simulator with NVIDIA cuQuantum backend. Through memory-efficient algorithms and optimized communication strategies, we achieve strong scalability for evaluating quantum algorithms toward fault-tolerant quantum computing.
