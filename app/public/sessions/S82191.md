# Benchmarking the Path to Useful Quantum Computers

## Speakers
### Robin Blume-Kohout (Presenter)
> Robin Blume-Kohout is the founder and codirector of Sandia's Quantum Performance Lab. A world-recognized expert in quantum computer benchmarking and characterization, he also serves on the editorial review board for the journal PRX Quantum.

## Abstract
Learn where quantum computing technologies are today, where they’re going, and how to track progress toward the goal of (someday) outperforming any supercomputer at solving useful problems. We’ll discuss how we assess and improve the performance of quantum computing hardware, the expanding role for HPC/GPUs/AI in this quest, and when it will (and won’t!) be useful to integrate HPC and GPUs with fault-tolerant quantum computers.
