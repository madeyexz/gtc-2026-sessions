## Abstract
This lab provides a hands-on deep dive into medical AI workflows for both clinical text and imaging, with a focus on building a synthetic patient record to address data scarcity and privacy challenges in healthcare.

You will first employ NeMo Data Designer to generate synthetic electronic health records (EHR), including patient visits, doctor notes, and follow-up reports. From there, you will use NVIDIA open models to handle segmentation, reasoning, and synthetic image generation for CT, MR, and X-ray data.

The lab provides an interactive viewer for immediate clinical validation and hands-on notebooks for deep technical customization. Whether you are exploring the provided samples or uploading your own images, we also provide ways to download sample data from the NIH to test on real-world data.

Each model contributes a different piece of the synthetic patient record, and by the end of the lab, you will understand how these tools fit together and be ready to explore the growing landscape of NVIDIA MedTech open models
