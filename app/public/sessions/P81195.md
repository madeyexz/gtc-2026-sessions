## Abstract
We present HMARCF-HILFOF, the first multi-agent agentic AI system integrated with NVIDIA Cosmos Physical AI for automated surgical robot training. By combining multi-expert debate reasoning with physics-based world simulation, we automate scenario design while ensuring clinical quality. HMARCF coordinates four agents — Thoracic Surgery, Robot Learning, Cosmos Engineering, and Imaging/Radiology — through structured debates to produce auditable, versioned Cosmos configurations (angles/tissue/dynamics/physics). HILFOF provides human-in-the-loop quality assurance with quantitative metrics (VACS >0.85, SBF >0.90) and feedback-driven regeneration. In preliminary phantom-based validation of thoracic needle insertion, the system expanded scenario diversity from 10 (manual baseline) to 30+ (5 angles × 3 tissues × 2 dynamics) while reducing prompt engineering time from hours to minutes.
