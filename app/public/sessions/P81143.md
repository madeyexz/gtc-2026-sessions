## Abstract
Our Prometheus framework for agentic engineering coordinates planning, coding, and deployment agents to automate software development. Explore the unification of human oversight and AI agents, in which we offer a new approach to the software development life cycle. Discover the benefits of our framework and the lessons we learned while working on it. 

The system consists of a multi-pronged approach. Planning involves gathering project requirements, business insights, and creating epics and tasks. We combine this with coding, frontend, backend and testing efforts, deployment, and verification.
