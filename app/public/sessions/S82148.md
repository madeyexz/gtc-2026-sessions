## Abstract
In this "Lightning Talks" session, industry experts will explore the evolving world of data analytics and streaming, covering AI-driven innovations in sponsorship analytics, data processing needs of streaming services, and real-time audience insights. Speakers will discuss how advanced data tools are driving end-to-end content automation and improving attribution accuracy, helping businesses optimize strategies and increase engagement. Gain actionable insights on the role of AI in refining analytics and enhancing operational efficiency in the streaming industry.
