# Latency Optimization in BugNeMo

`Poster` `Technical - Intermediate` `Developer Tools & Techniques`

## Speakers
### Jiaxiang Ren (Author)
> Jiaxiang works on agentic AI, Gen AI, and Text2SQL for NVIDIA.

## Abstract
High query latency hindered NVIDIA’s BugNeMo Text2SQL co-pilot operating on millions of records. We identified and resolved three key bottlenecks—table hint injection, inefficient LIKE searches, and missing indexes—through AST-based query rewriting, full-text indexing, and targeted index creation. These optimizations reduced median latency from 26.9 to 7.8 seconds, vastly improving responsiveness and scalability of the Gen AI-powered platform.
