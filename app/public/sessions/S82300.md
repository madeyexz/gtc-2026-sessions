## Abstract
Top leaders from Siemens and Volkswagen come together to explore how physical AI, Siemens’ industrial digitalization portfolio, and digital twins built on OpenUSD and NVIDIA Omniverse libraries are redefining the future of automated virtual commissioning. This executive conversation highlights how physics-accurate digital twins, real-time interoperability, and closed-loop automation are enabling faster engineering cycles, higher production quality, and more resilient manufacturing operations. 

The panel will examine how combining Siemens’ end-to-end automation and life-cycle platforms with real-time simulation, powered by NVIDIA libraries and accelerated AI infrastructure, creates a unified environment where design, controls, and operations continuously learn from each other. Together, Siemens and Volkswagen will share strategic insights on how these technologies are accelerating the shift from simulation to scalable, AI-driven production systems across global manufacturing networks.
