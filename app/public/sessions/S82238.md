## Abstract
As AI becomes the definitive engine of economic and academic growth, the race is on to democratize access to high performance computing across the United States. This panel explores "The 50-State Plan," a strategic roadmap for building robust, statewide AI infrastructure through innovative public-private partnerships. The session brings together visionary tech-industry pioneers, leaders from the National Science Foundation (NSF), and executives from top-tier research universities to discuss the shift from isolated pockets of innovation to unified strategies for university transformation and mission success in the age of AI.

Join us to learn how your organization can participate in this nationwide effort to bridge the digital divide and accelerate the next era of discovery.
