# Efficient On-Policy Distillation for Transferring Reasoning Capabilities ​via Parallel and Sparse Training​

> Shuang Yu is a solutions architect at NVIDIA, specializing in natural language processing, LLMs, and algorithm engineering. She holds a master's degree in computer science from Tsinghua University.

## Abstract
We present an efficient on-policy distillation framework enhanced by the novel Zero-trick, which mitigates logit alignment gaps in sparse distillation. Our work fills the gap of open-source on-policy distillation tools and drastically reduces computational overhead compared with full-logit distillation, making advanced reasoning capabilities more accessible for practical small-scale models.
