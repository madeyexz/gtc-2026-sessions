## Abstract
Get a hands-on introduction to NVIDIA CUDA-Q, the platform for seamlessly orchestrating both quantum and classical processors alongside each other in accelerated quantum supercomputers. Whether you are new to the field or an experienced developer, this session walks you through building a hybrid application to demonstrate the execution of quantum-classical workflows across NVIDIA GPUs and IQM’s quantum hardware.
