## Abstract
We present an AI-driven offline programming (OLP) and digital-twin framework for factory-level automation in automotive manufacturing. By integrating GPU-accelerated motion generation with NVIDIA Isaac Sim for simulation-based validation, we demonstrate how manufacturers can rapidly verify safety, cycle time, and precision before physical deployment, accelerating the digital transformation of production lines.
