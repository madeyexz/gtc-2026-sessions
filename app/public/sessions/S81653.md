## Abstract
Dive deep into why current large language models struggle to generate performant CUDA kernels for accelerated systems, despite being capable of functional code. We'll explore the latest advancements in benchmarking, agentic frameworks, and industry efforts that are building the necessary infrastructure to close this performance gap and enable you to write "speed of light" AI-coded CUDA.
