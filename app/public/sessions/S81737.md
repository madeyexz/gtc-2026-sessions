## Abstract
As developer approaches to in-game agentic AI become more advanced, standard off-the-shelf models often fall short. Game developers frequently seek expertise in developing and customizing the most suitable models for their specific applications, as well as optimizing these models for peak performance. In this session, our experts will demonstrate how we develop and evaluate language models with Agentic AI capabilities for gaming platforms. They will guide you on utilizing NVIDIA tools to assess, train, and fine-tune AI models specifically designed for your gaming applications.
