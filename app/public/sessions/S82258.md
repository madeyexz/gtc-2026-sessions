## Abstract
Join us to learn how to turn NVIDIA IGX and Holoscan into a reusable, medical-grade platform for real-time AI. We'll walk through the complete stack, from system architecture and safety concepts to Holoscan graph design and containerized SaMD deployment, with a focus on the decisions, trade-offs, and patterns you can apply to your own devices. The session emphasizes how to meet strict latency and reliability constraints in a pass-through video pipeline, how to design a common regulated platform that can host multiple AI applications, and how to enable external teams to build and ship models on top of that platform.
