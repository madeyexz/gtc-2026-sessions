## Abstract
Effective use of AI, accelerated computing, and advanced infrastructure is essential to drive research innovation and scientific breakthroughs. Researchers need to focus on sustainability, funding approaches, and strategic collaborations to maximize the impact of their work and fully leverage NVIDIA technologies.
  
This panel will provide successful research use cases of accelerated computing, showcasing how to better advocate and utilize research infrastructure available at academic institutions.
