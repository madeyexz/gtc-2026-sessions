## Abstract
This session explores how World of Tanks: HEAT plans to transition ML from research notebooks to live combat using NVIDIA Triton. We begin by defining the "strategic recommendation system" within a gaming context—covering data prep, model selection, and player-centric evaluation. From there, we dive into the infrastructure: why traditional in-process inference fails in high-intensity battles and how Triton standardizes deployment across CPU and GPU. Learn how we utilize ONNX, TensorRT, and dynamic batching to optimize high-frequency models and use Triton’s Model Analyzer to ensure peak performance before players hit the battlefield.
