> Nao engaged in research in the robotics and AI during graduate school, submitting a peer-reviewed paper to an international conference. After gaining development experience as a backend engineer, he now handles AI model development and various technical validations in the image domain at FastLabel.

## Abstract
We will explain how to implement data curation specialized for a specific domain (e.g., autonomous driving) by linking NVIDIA NeMo Curator and vision language models and utilizing image caption information. Solving the duplicate data problem, which is a bottleneck in AI development, and creating highly accurate datasets that are impossible to do manually are essential to improving model performance. This poster provides cutting-edge, practical insights that will dramatically improve AI learning efficiency and quality.
