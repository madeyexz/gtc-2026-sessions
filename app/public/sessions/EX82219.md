## Abstract
Physical AI is moving from research demos into real-world deployment. However, scaling Physical AI systems remains constrained by low-fidelity simulation without physics, limited data generation capacity, and the lack of large-scale, industrial-grade evaluation infrastructure. Lightwheel was built to close this gap, and trusted by 80% of leading Physical AI teams. Lightwheel is a simulation-driven Physical AI infrastructure company, providing the simulation, data, and evaluation platform required to train, scale, and deploy Physical AI systems in the real world.
