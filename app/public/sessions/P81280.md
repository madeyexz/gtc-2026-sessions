## Abstract
Colorectal cancer is a leading cause of cancer-related deaths. While virtual colonoscopy provides a noninvasive alternative, current approaches depend on manual segmentation and lack immersive exploration. We present ColonVR, a hybrid AI and virtual reality framework that combines NVIDIA MONAI with TotalSegmentator for automated colon reconstruction from unprepared CT scans. Our hybrid pipeline was benchmarked against NVIDIA VISTA 3D, MONAI, CLEAR, and TotalSegmentator, showing improved anatomical accuracy and surface continuity.

In a study with 30 participants, users searched for polyps inside a reconstructed colon in VR using three navigation modes: Automatic, Flythrough, and Teleport. Automatic navigation delivered the best trade-off between efficiency, accuracy, and comfort, highlighting how AI-driven reconstruction and immersive VR can advance diagnostic training.
