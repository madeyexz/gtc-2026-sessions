## Speakers
### Ely Greenfield (Presenter)
> Ely Greenfield is chief technology officer and senior vice president of Adobe’s Creative Products Group, Digital Media at Adobe. He oversees Creative Cloud—including flagship apps such as Photoshop, Premiere Pro, and Illustrator—as well as Adobe’s generative AI initiatives, including Adobe Firefly.

## Abstract
Learn how to design, train, and deploy large-scale custom generative AI models. This session will step you through data preparation, fine-tuning, and infrastructure management, and will highlight best practices for scaling large models efficiently. You’ll also see real-world examples with Adobe Firefly Foundry and NVIDIA NeMo integration that demonstrate how teams are applying these techniques to production-grade AI systems.
