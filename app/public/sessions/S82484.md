## Abstract
Autonomous ride-hailing is increasingly a daily reality for more people with Waymo. As the most mature manifestation of AI in the physical world, Waymo’s path has been defined by 200 million miles and over 20 million trips completed without a human specialist present. Drawing on this unparalleled scale of experience, we will explore Waymo’s approach to developing its own custom hardware, the vital role AI compute plays in enabling it, and how this integrated foundation makes our demonstrably safe AI architecture possible.
