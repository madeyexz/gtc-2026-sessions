## Abstract
Join Part 2 of this two-part curated session on what it takes to bring simulation-trained robotics into the real world. This session looks at how teams are translating behaviors developed in simulation into reliable clinical performance. We’ll cover practical approaches to closing the sim-to-real gap, along with the real challenges of deploying safe, robust, low-latency sensor-to-action systems in live healthcare environments.
