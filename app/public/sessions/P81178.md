## Abstract
Explore a plug-and-play FP8 training recipe that lets you train reasoning LLMs faster and with less memory—while matching BF16 accuracy—so you can scale context, batches, and experiments on the same budget. See how you can adopt it across pre-training, fine-tuning, and reinforcement learning (RL) to unlock more results per GPU hour. 

Our end-to-end FP8 pipeline improves speed and memory use without changing your model or schedule. Learn how to apply it today to do more with fixed compute budgets and deliver stronger downstream results.

We'll also share an FP8-first workflow that maintains accuracy while cutting compute and memory overhead. You get practical steps to drop it into your pre-training and post-training stacks.

Dive deep into an inclusive, end-to-end FP8 approach that turns training efficiency into real task gains.
