## Abstract
See how interactive 3D digital twins reduce retail store setup time by 70% and display costs by 40%, replacing expensive physical mock-ups with fully virtual environments. This self-improving platform integrates real-time inventory management, AI-driven planogram optimization, and demand forecasting — enabling over 7,200 stores to validate layouts, train employees, and test “what-if” scenarios safely before physical implementation.
