## Abstract
As vision AI continues to expand into real-world applications—from multi-modal systems like vision language models (VLMs) to depth-aware robotics using stereo vision—optimizing model performance on edge devices has become mission-critical. This talk dives into practical strategies for deploying high-performance vision models on constrained hardware. We'll explore techniques for customizing large-scale VLMs while maintaining multi-modal alignment, and optimizing depth estimation models for real-time stereo processing with limited compute and power budgets. You'll leave with a clear understanding of how to balance accuracy, latency, and resource usage when bringing advanced vision capabilities to the edge.
