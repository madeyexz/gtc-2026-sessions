## Abstract
We demonstrate how synthetic data generation and continuous learning solve the critical deployment barriers in water safety AI—reducing detection latency by 37% and cutting multi-site onboarding from two months to five days. Explore how generative AI mitigates model drift across diverse aquatic environments where labeled real-world data is scarce and costly.
