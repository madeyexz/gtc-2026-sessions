## Abstract
AI is transforming the world’s financial institutions from systems of record into systems of intelligence. In this session, we’ll explore why banks, asset managers, and payments firms are building AI “factories” that transform transactional exhaust into high‑value signals, powering everything from profitable trading and precision risk management to hyper‑personalized customer experiences and proactive fraud detection. We’ll discuss emerging patterns for domain‑adapted models, AI agents embedded in core banking and payments workflows, and the governance required to scale safely in a highly regulated environment. You'll leave with a practical roadmap for partnering with NVIDIA’s platforms and ecosystem to build AI‑native financial services that learn continuously and compete on intelligence, not just balance sheet size.
