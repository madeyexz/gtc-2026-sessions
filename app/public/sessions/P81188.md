## Abstract
We present CudaqToEinsum, a high-performance framework for converting quantum circuits into Tensor-network representations. Integrated tightly with CUDA Quantum, our method achieves 30–50× faster translation than the Qiskit + cuQuantum CircuitToEinsum route on 85,000-qubit BPS circuits. With native support for batched contraction, it processes over 150,000 samples simultaneously, achieving up to 200–300× speedups, paving the way for large-scale quantum simulations on HPC platforms.
