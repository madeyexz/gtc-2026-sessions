## Abstract
Safety, cybersecurity, and AI safety are interconnected mission-critical properties of physical AI. As autonomous systems move into the real world, they require a foundation of trust spanning from sensors and hardware platforms to operating systems, reasoning models, and agentic functions. 

Join us to explore the NVIDIA Halos safety framework and learn how to architect certifiable systems for Level 4 autonomous vehicles and robotics. We’ll examine the design of robust hardware and software safety foundations—integrating hardware platform and operating system—to ensure rigorous safety and systematic cybersecurity by design across the entire stack.  You'll learn how to leverage NVIDIA DRIVE Hyperion for automotive, and NVIDIA IGX for robotics, to implement advanced inside-out and outside-in functional safety use cases for scalable autonomy.
