## Abstract
Dive deep into the engineering of Swallow, Japan’s sovereign AI, where we trained from Qwen3 and GPT-OSS using Megatron-LM with custom datasets like SwallowMath-v2 and SwallowCode-v2. Learn how our pipeline of continual pre-training, supervised fine-tuning (SFT), and reinforcement learning (RL) achieves high Japanese proficiency while preserving the original models’ reasoning capabilities, offering a scalable framework for sovereign AI development in other non-English languages.
