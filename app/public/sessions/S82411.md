## Speakers
### Illia Polosukhin (Presenter)
> Illia Polosukhin is co-founder of NEAR, on the mission to build user-owned AI. Before NEAR, Illia worked on AI research at Google and co-authored the landmark paper, "Attention Is All You Need."

## Abstract
AI agents are emerging as significant economic actors, yet today's markets are designed for human participants, burdened by cognitive load, irrational behavior and biases, and a high-friction legal paradigm. Illia introduces a formal model for agentic markets where autonomous AI agents directly express and match intents. He proposes a new set of agentic market primitives including programmatic escrow, agentic dispute resolution, and intent‑based matching and settlement. This paradigm shift necessitates revising classical economic problems for agentic systems.
