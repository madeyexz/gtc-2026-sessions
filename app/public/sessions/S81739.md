## Abstract
Ubisoft recently unveiled Teammates, its first playable AI-based R&D project. This experience redefines NPC interaction by allowing players to lead a proactive, intelligent squad via real-time voice commands. In this session, the developers behind Teammates will detail their process for selecting and tuning AI models to maximize player immersion. They will also provide a technical deep dive into their deployment strategy for high-performance, real-time inference.
