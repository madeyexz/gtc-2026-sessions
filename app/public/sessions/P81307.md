## Abstract
NVIDIA employees face the real problem of information overload on a daily basis. Communication, project updates, knowledge sharing, and cross-functional collaborations are spread across multiple third-party enterprise cloud providers such as Teams, Outlook, Confluence, Jira, Slack, etc. People spend a lot of time sifting through noisy information.

We propose an AI-based "Personal Assistant" to automatically connect to people's information sources, leveraging AI to identify people's ongoing tasks, to-dos, recent accomplishments, etc. We propose an MCP-based architecture for our app, where the Personal Assistant operates as an MCP client, while leveraging native or partner-hosted MCP servers to tackle various daily task that requires various data access. We also set ourselves as an example of standard MCP-based agentic architecture with a high standard on security.
