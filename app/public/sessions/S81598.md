## Abstract
Join Rev Lebaredian, NVIDIA’s VP of Omniverse and simulation technology, as he shares how autonomous, OpenClaw-based agents, digital twins and real-time simulation—powered by OpenUSD and NVIDIA’s accelerated computing platform, libraries, and ecosystem—are accelerating breakthroughs across industries in the era of physical AI. Discover their impact on everything from product design and manufacturing to robotics, autonomous vehicles, smart cities, climate science, and space exploration.
