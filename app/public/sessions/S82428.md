## Abstract
Open models are rapidly evolving beyond text-only understanding to reason across documents, images, and video. In this session, we trace the technical evolution of the NVIDIA Nemotron family from early text-focused models to multi-modal models. We explore architectural decisions and trade-offs behind each phase. We also share how open backbones, combined with open datasets and post-training recipes, enable developers to build multi-modal models.
