## Abstract
Astra Blueprints enables one-click deployment of agentic AI workflows on on-premises NVIDIA infrastructure using the NeMo Agent Toolkit. The framework provides blueprint-based templates to standardize workflow creation and configuration while supporting customization across use cases. With continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD) and GitOps automation, Astra orchestrates code, image, and deployment life cycles via the Astra Console, integrating with Vault, ArgoCD, and JFrog Artifactory. OpenTelemetry ensures unified observability with real-time metrics, traces, and logs. By abstracting infrastructure complexity and ensuring enterprise-grade scalability, privacy, and compliance, Astra Blueprints accelerates the transition from prototype to production—empowering teams to deploy and monitor multi-agent AI systems efficiently.
