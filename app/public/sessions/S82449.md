## Abstract
Join leaders from NVIDIA and Universal Music Group for a conversation exploring how technology innovation accelerated by artificial intelligence is reshaping the future of music and creative industries. The history of music transformation through technology over the course of one of the music industry's most influential careers will set the stage, with details about Sir Lucian’s experience engaging with artists on tech innovation and a focus on his vision for an AI partnership where technology and creativity can work together at scale. Moderated by Richard Kerris, general manager of Media2 at NVIDIA, former CTO of Lucasfilm, and previous global lead for Apple’s entertainment industry developer ecosystem, the discussion will offer candid insights and real-world use cases on how collaboration between tech and culture leaders is defining the next era of entertainment.
