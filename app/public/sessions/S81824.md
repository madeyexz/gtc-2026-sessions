## Abstract
NVIDIA CUDA Direct Sparse Solver (cuDSS) is increasingly being adopted in large-scale simulations for engineering and design, such as those pertinent to electronic engineering automation (EDA) and computer-aided engineering (CAE). In this session, we will cover recent advances, including most recent performance number, highlights of key features, and new APIs in cuDSS that enable users across various workflows to leverage GPU acceleration for sparse solvers.
