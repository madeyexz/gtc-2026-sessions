## Abstract
Creating truly scalable AI solutions for cities is a massive challenge, one that requires many AI models and platforms (and tons of data) working in harmony to make it possible. Announced at GTC Paris, NVIDIA Blueprint for smart city AI leverages 3-computer to activate physical AI and apply to cities for a unified, real-time intelligence solution for creating a smart city. Learn about the latest workflow updates. Linker Vision will share how they've been able to apply the blueprint with U.S. cities, from training models with synthetic data and NVIDIA Cosmos to deploying video analytics AI agents using NVIDIA Metropolis Blueprint for Video Search and Summarization (VSS) that leverages the fleet of camera sensors deployed in cities—an end-to-end approach to processing tens of thousands of camera streams for proactive automation of city operations, safety, and planning.
