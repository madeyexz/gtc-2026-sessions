## Abstract
This talk explores the Ozaki Scheme, including its two variants, as a practical approach for recovering high accuracy from low-precision computations in numerical linear algebra.
Participation is important as it sheds light on how we can effectively respond to the accuracy challenges posed by next-generation accelerator architectures.
