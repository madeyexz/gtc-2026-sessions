## Abstract
Retail brands are under pressure to produce high-quality content at unprecedented speed and scale without increasing costs, and this session demonstrates how AI-powered digital twin technology built with NVIDIA Omniverse addresses that challenge. By transforming physical products into hyper-realistic, physics-accurate virtual assets, retailers can instantly deploy them across marketing, advertising, and e-commerce. Using robotic capture systems pre-trained with NVIDIA Isaac Sim and AI lighting agents trained with NVIDIA Kaolin, products are converted into production-grade digital twins with optimized illumination and material realism. Real-world case studies illustrate how workflows built with Omniverse enable mass content creation, synthetic data generation, and real-time rendering—replacing traditional photoshoots with scalable, AI-driven pipelines that deliver measurable improvements in speed, efficiency, and performance.
