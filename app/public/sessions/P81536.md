## Abstract
By implementing reduced precision, we improved end-to-end simulation throughput by factors ranging from 1.8x to ~12x across a variety of interatomic potentials in the LAMMPS molecular dynamics code, as measured on an RTX Pro 6000 Blackwell Server Edition GPU.
