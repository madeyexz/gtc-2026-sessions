## Abstract
In this session you will learn how NVIDIA’s reference voice applications and models are developed to establish new state-of-the-art accuracy numbers on multiple benchmarks and user experience. This session covers the standard voice application pipeline configuration consisting of Nemotron Speech Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR), language models (LLM), and speech synthesis (TTS), and advanced configurations for end-of-utterance detection and barge-in or backchannelling handling. It will also cover how end-to-end voice models are making voice application development more user friendly and easy to deploy and maintain. Voice model development including pre-training techniques as well as advanced speech alignment techniques to turn base models into state-of-the-art instruct models will also be covered. We will also share best practices for voice agent and model development for various domains and applications.
