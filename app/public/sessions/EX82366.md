## Abstract
U.S. electricity demand is rising fast. But connecting new energy sources to the grid is challenging, with twice the total generated power capacity (1.28 terawatts) in the United States sitting in a "queue," waiting for interconnection. This backlog is largely due to the exhaustive, manual simulation studies required to ensure that new power sources do not destabilize the existing grid. This session highlights how Hitachi and NVIDIA are using physical AI to reduce the time taken to connect new energy sources to the grid by up to 80%.
