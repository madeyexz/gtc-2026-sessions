## Abstract
Sharing lessons from the training of SWE-1.6.  Preview which beat top open-source models on SWE-Bench Pro. We will cover RL environments for agentic coding, a stable algorithm for async RL, how we made our training 6x faster with NVFP4 & more, and how we approach GPU allocation for async RL.
