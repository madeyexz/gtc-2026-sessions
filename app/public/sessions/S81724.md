## Abstract
The automotive industry is rapidly pivoting toward a future where intelligence inside the vehicle defines brand differentiation, customer experience, and long-term value. Breakthroughs in GenAI, cloud–edge orchestration, and accelerated computing are enabling automakers to deliver safer, smarter, and more intuitive experiences—turning every vehicle into a continuously improving digital product. In this panel we will share how leaders across different companies in the ecosystem - AI infrastructure, cloud, Tier-1 platforms, and next-generation EV OEMs - are shaping the in-vehicle GenAI landscape. The session offers a view of where the industry is headed and what it will take to bring truly intelligent, AI-powered vehicles to market at global scale.
