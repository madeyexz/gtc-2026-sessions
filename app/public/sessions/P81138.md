## Abstract
We explore how small language models (SLMs) running on small devices integrate with NVIDIA NIM microservices running in the cloud or local GPU environments to enable real-time, hybrid AI inference. Our hybrid local–cloud inference approach combines on-device processing for instant interaction with GPU-accelerated reasoning in the cloud. This solution achieves latency under 200 ms and is expected to significantly reduce bandwidth usage — potentially by up to 10× — while scaling across industries from consumer electronics to healthcare and retail. Built with NVIDIA technologies including NeMo, TensorRT, and Triton, we demonstrate how hybrid inference brings AI closer to the user, powering natural, multi-modal experiences directly on devices.
