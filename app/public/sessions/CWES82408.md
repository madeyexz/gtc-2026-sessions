## Abstract
NVIDIA-Certified Systems provide the essential foundation for the modern AI factory, offering performance-optimized hardware validated to run demanding workloads from the desktop to the data center and edge. This session explores how the program’s rigorous testing—now including NVIDIA-Certified Storage for massive data demands—ensures enterprise-grade reliability, security, and scalability for generative AI and industrial digitalization. Join us to learn how these certified servers, workstations, and edge systems provide a trusted, best-practice pathway for enterprises to confidently deploy accelerated computing at scale.

Important: Connect With the Experts sessions are interactive sessions that give you a unique opportunity to meet, in either a group or one-on-one setting, with the minds behind NVIDIA’s products and research to get your questions answered. Attendees can meet with experts on a first-come, first-served basis.
