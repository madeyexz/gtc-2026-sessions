## Abstract
Our framework accelerates development, training, and deployment of intelligent robotic systems by integrating digital model creation, synthetic data generation, real-time scene adaptation, LLMs, and vision language models (VLMs). Uing NVIDIA technologies for high-performance simulation and inference enables realistic training and testing of vision models and overall system performance. LLM and VLM integration allows the system to interpret user commands, generate executable code, and enhance scene understanding for intuitive human-machine collaboration. A custom vision system provides 3D data coordinates for robot manipulation in the simulation. The system is already used for industrial purposes, including high-fidelity perception for manufacturing, quality control, and surveillance. Results from simulated and real-world deployments demonstrate the framework's adaptability and potential.
