## Abstract
Roughly 70% of AI projects fail to transition to production due to challenges related to data compliance and security. To address this challenge, enterprise storage systems are evolving from data delivery to knowledge and intelligence delivery systems. Join us to learn how NVIDIA is collaborating with leading partners from the industry on architectures that deliver AI-ready data to accelerate the path from proof of concept to production.
