## Abstract
This work presents a fast and accurate GPU-accelerated virtual drug screening pipeline. In the first stage, a GPU-annealer was built to simulate a quantum annealer and used for molecular docking, achieving faster performance with competitive AUC and EF1. In the second stage, CUDA-Q VQE refined binding energies for reliable ranking and better EF1. During the entire pipeline, GPU delivers up to four orders of magnitude speedup over CPU execution, accelerating early drug discovery.
