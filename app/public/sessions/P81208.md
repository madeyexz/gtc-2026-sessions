## Abstract
We present a hybrid AI agent that combines retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) and LoRA-tuned models to achieve 95.7% accuracy in generating transportation planning commands from natural language, reducing technical barriers and letting planners focus on analytical insights instead of memorizing command-line interface (CLI) syntax.
