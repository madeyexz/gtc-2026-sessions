## Abstract
Robotics and physical AI have reached their “ChatGPT” moment. The reliability for industrial use requires precision dexterity, beginning with precision simulation and high sim2real fidelity, where robots learn and reason in simulation before deployment. This session highlights the collaboration between Synopsys and Analog Devices (ADI) to augment the fidelity of simulation environments for dexterity in industrial robotics. The collaboration includes an industry-standard benchmark for tactile manipulation, led by ADI and enabled by Synopsys.
