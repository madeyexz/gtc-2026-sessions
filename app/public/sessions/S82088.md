## Abstract
The convergence of AI and advanced simulation technologies is transforming how industries design, build, and operate both products and facilities. This session explores how NVIDIA Omniverse libraries and OpenUSD-based digital twins are enabling Schneider Electric to drive a new era of AI-driven simulations and smart buildings across all sectors—from manufacturing and energy to data centers and AI factories. Learn how these technologies create interoperable, scalable, and virtual environments that accelerate innovation, optimize workflows, and unlock unprecedented collaboration. Discover how AI factories and intelligent infrastructure can leverage real-time simulation to reduce costs, improve efficiency, and drive continuous improvement. Join us to understand why open standards and immersive simulation ecosystems are the foundation for the future of AI.
