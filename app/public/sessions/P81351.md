## Abstract
The Agentic Scrum Master (ASM) is a multi-service platform using the NVIDIA NeMo Agent Toolkit to orchestrate workflows autonomously.

Its intelligent core integrates five applications/systems that provide proactive insights like predictive risk assessment and Smart assignee recommendations.

ASM achieves true portfolio alignment via full bidirectional Jira-Jira Align linking. This autonomously manages epic-to-capability linking and feature hierarchies, solving a major enterprise coordination problem. ASM's development was lead by a Scrum master in the Agentic AI Hackathon at NVIDIA in 2025. A non-developer picked it up and enhanced it further, inspiring others use it to reduce the admin churn and move from reactive scrum mastering to proactive strategic leadership to deliver highest value to customers.
