## Abstract
Explore why more enterprises are shifting AI workloads closer to protect sensitive company data and how to operationalize Private AI without sacrificing performance. In this session, we’ll break down the real-world drivers behind on-prem and private deployments (data sovereignty, IP protection, regulated workloads, and risk reduction), then walk through a practical blueprint for running modern models with full control of data. Attendees will leave with a clear framework for model selection, secure deployment design, and operational controls so teams can confidently deploy high-performance inference in private environments while keeping sensitive data in place and minimizing exposure.
