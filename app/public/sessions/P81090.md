# Continuous-Time NODE in Transformers and Physics-Informed Loss as Soft Physical Constraints in Weather Forecasting

`Poster` `Technical - Intermediate` `Simulation / Modeling / Design`

## Speakers
### Hira Saleem (Author)
> Hira is a Ph.D. candidate at UNSW holding the SmartSat CRC scholarship. Her research interests include AI for weather and climate, explainable AI, spatio-temporal forecasting, and computer vision in general.

## Abstract
We develop a novel approach to integrate continuous-time Neural ODE dynamics to transformers and implement physics-informed loss as soft physical constraints to learn the spatio-temporal mapping of weather variables in the embedding space of the vision transformer. As climate volatility grows, this approach offers timely improvements in forecast quality without sacrificing transformer's learning capacity.
