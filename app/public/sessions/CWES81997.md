## Abstract
Discover how NVIDIA developed the Nemotron family of edge-ready models, setting new benchmarks for accuracy and performance. This session explores standard pre-training to advanced pruning and distillation for edge-scale efficiency. We will also detail the alignment techniques used to create state-of-the-art multimodal reasoning models. Finally, learn best practices for domain adaptation and the deployment of customized Nemotron models for gaming and AI applications on GeForce RTX platforms.
