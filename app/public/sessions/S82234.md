## Abstract
AI is moving from experimentation to global production, and inference efficiency and quality are the defining competitive edge. This session dives deep into the engineering behind high-performance, open-model inference, covering the system decisions, runtime techniques, and architectural patterns that unlock predictable, real-world performance at scale. Learn how Nebius Token Factory and NVIDIA push beyond benchmark-driven results to deliver structural, compounding gains in latency, throughput, and cost.
