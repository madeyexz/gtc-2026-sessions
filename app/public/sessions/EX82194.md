## Abstract
Enterprises and governments are rethinking how they deploy large foundation models alongside smaller, domain-specific language models to achieve accuracy, security, and cost efficiency at scale. While foundation models provide broad intelligence, domain-tuned small language models (SLMs) are increasingly critical for embedding AI into vertical, regulated, and low-latency operational workflows. 

In this session, we explore how Tech Mahindra is leveraging NVIDIA-accelerated platforms to support the evolution from foundation models to production-grade AI factories—systems that continuously train, fine-tune, deploy, and operate both LLMs and SLMs. Drawing on real-world experience, including telecom-specific language models delivering measurable operational outcomes—such as AI-led handling of over 50% of network operations tickets across autonomous and human-approved workflows—and India’s Indus AI mission focused on education at national scale, we show how sovereign AI, private AI, and edge AI can be integrated to deliver secure, scalable, and vertical-ready AI solutions, leveraging NVIDIA technologies such as NVIDIA AI Enterprise, RAPIDS, and NeMo-based data tooling.
