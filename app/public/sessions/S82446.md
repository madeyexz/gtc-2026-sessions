## Abstract
Simulation remains a cornerstone for accelerating robotics and autonomous vehicle (AV) development, enabling policies to be evaluated ahead of real-world deployment and drastically reducing development time. Achieving meaningful results requires simulators that capture both the environment and the world’s response to AI agents with high fidelity, across diverse and challenging scenarios. NVIDIA Cosmos has been specialized for autonomous vehicles, incorporating novel architecture changes and optimizations to deliver real-time simulation for the first time. In this talk, we explore the breakthrough that powers closed-loop evaluation of AV policies, empowering developers to iterate faster, test more rigorously, and bring safer, more robust autonomous systems to the real world.
