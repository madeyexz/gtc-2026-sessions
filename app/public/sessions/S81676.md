## Abstract
The integration of physical and digital robotics into operating rooms (ORs) is revolutionizing surgical care, driving precision, efficiency, and improved patient outcomes. Join us for an insightful panel discussion as we explore cutting-edge advancements in intelligent surgical robotics systems. We'll examine technological breakthroughs in digital robotic training through physics-based simulation, autonomous surgical assistance, and real-time edge AI deployment in the OR. We'll explore how these advancements empower surgeons with enhanced precision while reducing cognitive load. You'll hear unique perspectives on the future of the operating room and how physical and digital robotics enable unprecedented surgical excellence.
