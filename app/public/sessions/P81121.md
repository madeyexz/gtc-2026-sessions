`Poster` `Technical - Beginner` `Agentic AI / Generative AI`

## Speakers
### Atikan Payungthong (Author)

## Abstract
We explore new techniques for fine-tuning open-source multi-modal models like Qwen, LLaMA, and Gemma to understand Thai language and culture. By combining curated Thai data from the internet, university courses, and volunteer contributions, we aim to improve model performance and enable AI applications for education, travel, call-center services, and research
