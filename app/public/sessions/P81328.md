## Abstract
This poster presents the first GPU-accelerated EEG-to-Chinese text generation framework, enabling brain-to-text communication for Chinese speakers with speech disorders. By leveraging the NVIDIA H100’s Transformer Engine and mixed-precision training, the system achieves threefold faster training and 60 TFLOPS performance, making real-time decoding feasible. The model decodes 128-channel EEG signals into open-vocabulary Chinese text, addressing a long-standing gap in multilingual brain-computer interface (BCI) research. This work empowers inclusive assistive communication and sets a new foundation for large-scale, clinically deployable Chinese brain-computer interfaces.
