## Abstract
As brands scale personalized campaigns across channels and regions, traditional production models break down. This session explores how teams automate content creation with 3D digital twins and generative AI—transforming physical products into reusable systems that power high-volume pipelines.

Learn how automated workflows generate packshots, composite imagery, and product configurations at scale, replacing manual photoshoots with cloud-native, production-ready content pipelines.
