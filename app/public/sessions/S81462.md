## Abstract
Alibaba Cloud ACK RBG is a management tool for the full life cycle of large model inference deployment on Kubernetes, designed to address the challenges of large-scale deployment and operations of prefill-decode (PD) disaggregation architecture within Kubernetes clusters. It not only provides features such as multi-role management/collaboration, auto service discovery, topology-aware placement, elastic scaling, and atomic rollout/failure recovery, but also tightly integrates with NVIDIA Dynamo's AIConfigurator and Planner to achieve performance profiling and smarter scaling in complex PD disaggregation and mixture-of-experts (MoE) scenarios. This aims to further improve inference efficiency and reduce costs while meeting service level agreement (SLA) requirements, representing one of the effective ways for large-scale practical deployment of NVIDIA Dynamo.
