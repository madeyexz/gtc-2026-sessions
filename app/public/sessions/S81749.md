## Abstract
Join XRlabs and Olympus for a session exploring how technologies such as NVIDIA Isaac for Healthcare are shaping innovation in surgical environments. The discussion will include an overview of Olympus' 3D 4K Exoscope technology, as well as XRlabs' simulation and model training expertise, highlighting how AI-powered workflows can help bridge the gap between digital and physical environments. You'll see how simulation, digital twins, and robotics are being used to enable safer procedures, faster training, and smarter decision-making in the operating room. From modeling complex anatomy to validating robotic tasks in virtual environments before potential deployment in patient care, this session will explore emerging approaches that may influence the future of medical device development and precision surgery.
