## Abstract
For two decades, the "self-healing system" was just a theory. Today, it's an architectural reality. This session unveils the blueprint for the fully autonomous AI factory—a cognitive system where fleets of specialized agents replace static operations such as issues detection and troubleshooting to deliver true self-governance.

We'll move beyond the hype of simple chatbots to deconstruct a multi-agent architecture capable of complex reasoning, predictive failure analysis, and continuous self-improvement. But we won’t just tell you about it; we will show you.

This session features a high-stakes LIVE DEMO of an autonomous multi-agent swarm connected to a large-scale production system. Watch in real time as agents detect (and predict) anomalies and use advanced tools for troubleshooting as the first self-healing AI platform for autonomous AI factories.
