## Abstract
AI is fundamentally transforming real-time applications, reshaping both content creation and player experiences. Join John Spitzer, VP of developer technology and performance at NVIDIA, for an industry panel featuring AI leaders from Creative Assembly, Decart, RIOT Games and Krafton. These panelists will explore the current state of the art—from AI digital humans to generating entire worlds instantaneously—and offer their vision for the future of AI-powered gaming.
