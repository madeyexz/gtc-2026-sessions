## Abstract
Algorithmic trading is entering a new era defined by AI, massive data volumes, and unprecedented compute scale. Many leading firms now operate more like advanced AI labs than traditional trading desks, relying on large fleets of NVIDIA GPUs and consuming petabytes of real-time and historical market data. The main barrier is no longer the models, but accessing and processing the data fast enough to power them. 

This session traces how the industry has evolved over the past 20 years, where firms are finding real success with AI/ML, and what frontiers are emerging as compute and data converge. We’ll show how new approaches to data delivery, storage, and accelerated networking, together with solutions from Databento and NVIDIA, are opening the door to the next wave of innovation in finance.
