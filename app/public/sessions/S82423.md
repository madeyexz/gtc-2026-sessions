## Abstract
The future of enterprise AI is shaped by how intelligence is designed, deployed, and scaled across the enterprise. Next-gen computing platforms enable advanced models, including generative and agentic AI, to operate reliably in complex environments, allowing organizations to move beyond isolated automation toward AI-driven systems that reason, learn, and act in real time. 

This session examines how enterprises are designing AI-native architectures that combine high-performance compute, modern data platforms, and advanced models to support production-grade AI at scale. It explores how governance, trust, and resilience are embedded into AI systems from Day 1. With examples from industries such as banking, financial services, telecommunications, and media, the discussion highlights how accelerated AI is transforming enterprises from software-driven execution to intelligence-driven outcomes.
