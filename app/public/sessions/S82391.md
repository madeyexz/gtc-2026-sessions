## Abstract
The era of agentic AI is upon us. Generative AI and agentic architectures are no longer novelties—they’re rapidly becoming core infrastructure in leading enterprises. In this panel, moderated by NVIDIA’s vice president of enterprise AI, leading chief AI officers, CIOs, and AI platform leaders from NVIDIA, customers will share candid perspectives on building, scaling, and governing AI platforms and agentic systems in real-world settings. They will discuss how to move beyond pilot projects to large-scale adoption, manage infrastructure and inference pipelines, integrate agents into business workflows, and maintain trust, transparency, and resilience. Join us to hear from those who are navigating the challenges of enterprise AI transformation at scale.
