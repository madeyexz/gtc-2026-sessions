## Abstract
Learn how the integration of NVIDIA's accelerated computing and Planet Labs' cloud-scale imagery platform is driving a revolutionary shift in geospatial workflows at scale and in real time. This integration of Earth observation innovation pioneered by Planet, NVIDIA’s platform, and AI unlocks the full potential of petabytes of Earth observation data, providing detailed, actionable insights that address some of the world’s most pressing environmental and humanitarian challenges.
