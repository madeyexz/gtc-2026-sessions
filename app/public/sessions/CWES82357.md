## Abstract
Stop by our session to explore video workflows accelerated by NVIDIA's dedicated video encoders (NVENC) and decoders (NVDEC). GPUs with dedicated video codecs power AI video pipelines, training, and inference alongside gaming, professional video production, streaming, and data center applications. 

Product managers, engineers, solution architects, and DevTech experts will be on hand to dive into our video feature sets, troubleshoot pipelines, or answer any video-related questions you have.
