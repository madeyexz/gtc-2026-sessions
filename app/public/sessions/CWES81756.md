## Abstract
The future of digital health is agentic. Join NVIDIA experts to discuss how AI agents are transforming the ecosystem by bridging the gap between perception and reasoning. Discuss the implementation of advanced pipelines that utilize conversational AI and speech for patient interaction, computer vision for diagnostics, and deep analytics for decision support. Whether you are a researcher or an enterprise developer, engage with our team to understand how NVIDIA NIM, blueprints, and SDKs can help you deploy robust, multi-modal agents that drive real-world clinical, research, revenue cycle, and administrative outcomes.

Important: Connect With the Experts sessions are interactive sessions that give you a unique opportunity to meet, in either a group or one-on-one setting, with the minds behind NVIDIA’s products and research to get your questions answered.
