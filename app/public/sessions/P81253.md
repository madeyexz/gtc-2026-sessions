## Abstract
Numerical simulation of the Navier-Stokes equation is among the most complex problems in computational sciences. The complexity increases tremendously when incorporating moving boundaries and modeling interactions with multiple physics, such as structural mechanics, acoustics, and more. We present a sharp-interface immersed boundary method and accelerate it using GPUs to simulate some of the most complex problems currently beyond the capabilities of many industrial solvers.
