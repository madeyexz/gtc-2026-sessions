## Abstract
This panel brings together industry leaders from Honeywell Industrial, KION Group, Caterpillar, Medtronic, Hitachi Rail and GXO to explore how advanced AI and edge computing are reshaping the future of industrial and healthcare systems. Together, they will discuss the major trends accelerating edge intelligence—from autonomous operations and multi-modal sensor fusion to real-time decision-making and digital twins—and share high-impact AI use cases driving measurable business growth and societal value. The conversation will highlight challenges in deploying scalable, safety-critical edge AI and reveal how these global innovators are architecting their next-generation solutions using NVIDIA’s accelerated computing and end-to-end edge platform.
