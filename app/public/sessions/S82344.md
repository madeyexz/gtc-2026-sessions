## Abstract
Building AI-native applications is fundamentally different from traditional software—and scaling them demands infrastructure designed for models, not just compute. Decagon builds AI agents for enterprise customer support across voice, chat, and email, backed by a research org that trains and fine-tunes specialized models to execute complex workflows through Agent Operating Procedures. As Decagon scaled, running autonomous agents reliably and economically became the defining challenge—especially for real-time voice, where silence reads as failure and latency is instantly perceptible. In this session, learn how Together AI, the AI-native cloud, helped Decagon turn a multi-model agent stack into a production system that meets sub-second latency targets and stays reliable through traffic surges.
