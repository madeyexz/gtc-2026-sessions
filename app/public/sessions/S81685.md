## Abstract
To meet the growing demand for power of AI, we have to enable the entire ecosystem, from chip to grid. This lightning talk brings together how NVIDIA is working with its partners from power generation and transmission to distribution to build flexible and reliable infrastructure. This session will focus on use of AI for meeting energy demand, using digital technologies inside data centers to manage peak loads, and applying accelerated computing to simulate the grid and build physical infrastructure.
