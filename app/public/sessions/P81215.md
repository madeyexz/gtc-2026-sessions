# LFQUIAD: Lookup-Free Quantized Autoencoder for Few-Shot Unsupervised Industrial Anomaly Detection 

`Poster` `Technical - Intermediate` `Computer Vision / Video Analytics`

## Speakers
### SHIHCHIH LIN (Author)
> With 20-plus years in AI and smart manufacturing, Shih-Chih focuses on computer vision and anomaly detection for semiconductor automation. A PMP, PMI-ACP, and PMI-PBA professional and AIA Executive Program graduate, he bridges industry and research, with papers published in ECCVW, ICCVW, CVPRW, BMVC...

## Abstract
Explore how we generate realistic synthetic anomalies using diffusion-based inpainting to train a quantized autoencoder without relying on large codebooks. Learn how our LFQUIAD framework improves anomaly detection and segmentation with minimal real data, offering a scalable and plug-and-play solution for industrial and medical inspection.
